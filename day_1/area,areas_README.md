### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   (None commonly used in academic English)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Area
    *   Areas (plural)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Areal (less common)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   (None)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Area (Noun):**
    *   **English:** A specific part of a place, a subject, or a space.
    *   **Roman Urdu:** Kisi jagah ka hissa, ya phir kisi khas subject ka field. Jese hum kehte hain "Computer Science ka area."
*   **Areas (Noun - Plural):**
    *   **English:** More than one area.
    *   **Roman Urdu:** Ek se zyada jagah ya fields.
*   **Areal (Adjective - less common):**
    *   **English:** Related to an area.
    *   **Roman Urdu:** Jo kisi khas area se talluq rakhta ho.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Region:** (Ilaka)
*   **Field:** (Khas mazmoon ya shoba)
*   **Zone:** (Khas hissa ya area)

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Area:**
    1.  Artificial Intelligence is a growing area of research.
        *   (Artificial Intelligence research ka ek barhta hua shoba/area hai.)
    2.  The memory area stores temporary data for the processor.
        *   (Memory ka area processor ke liye temporary data store karta hai.)

*   **Areas:**
    1.  We need to focus on two different areas of this project.
        *   (Hamein is project ke do alag areas par tawajo dene ki zaroorat hai.)
    2.  Network security covers many technical areas.
        *   (Network security bohat se technical areas ko cover karti hai.)

*   **Areal (less common):**
    1.  The study looks at the areal distribution of servers.
        *   (Yeh study servers ki areal (jagah ke mutabiq) taqseem ko dekhti hai.)
    2.  Areal coverage is important for wireless signals.
        *   (Wireless signals ke liye areal coverage (jagah ki pohnch) zaroori hai.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1.  Cybersecurity is a vital area in modern computing. (Cybersecurity modern computing mein ek ahem area hai.)
2.  Research in the area of robotics is moving fast. (Robotics ke area mein research tezi se chal rahi hai.)
3.  We identified three main areas for software improvement. (Hum ne software behtar banane ke liye teen main areas ki nishandahi ki.)
4.  The storage area of the hard drive is full. (Hard drive ka storage area bhara hua hai.)
5.  Data science covers many different academic areas. (Data science bohat se mukhtalif academic areas ko cover karta hai.)
6.  Please define the area of your study clearly. (Barah-e-karam apni study ka area wazeh taur par define karein.)
7.  The system scans all areas for potential viruses. (System tamam areas ko potential viruses ke liye scan karta hai.)
8.  Which area of programming do you like most? (Aap ko programming ka kaun sa area sab se zyada pasand hai?)
9.  The database covers a wide area of information. (Database maloomat ke ek wasee area ko cover karta hai.)
10. We are working on the interface area of the app. (Hum app ke interface area par kaam kar rahe hain.)
11. Are there specific areas where the code fails? (Kya koi khas areas hain jahan code fail ho jata hai?)
12. The areal extent of the network is quite large. (Network ka areal phailao kafi bara hai.)
13. Focus your research on a narrow area first. (Pehle apni research ko ek chote area par markooz karein.)
14. Cloud computing is a popular area for students. (Cloud computing students ke liye ek maqbool area hai.)
15. The system monitors all sensitive areas of the network. (System network ke tamam sensitive areas ki nigrani karta hai.)
