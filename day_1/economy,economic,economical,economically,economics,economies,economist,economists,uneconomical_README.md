Hello! I am your English instructor. Today, we are going to learn about the word family **"Economy."** This is a very important word in academic studies, especially when we talk about money, resources, and computer science projects.

Let’s start!

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Economize (to save money)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Economy (the system of money/resources)
    *   Economics (the study of money)
    *   Economist (a person who studies money)
    *   Economies (plural of economy)
    *   Economists (plural of economist)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Economic (related to money/business)
    *   Economical (using money/resources carefully)
    *   Uneconomical (not worth the money; wasteful)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   Economically (in a way that saves money)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Economize:** To use less money or resources. (Paisa ya resources bachanay ki koshish karna.)
*   **Economy:** The system of how a country or business uses money. (Mulki ya karobari nizam-e-maal.)
*   **Economics:** The subject that studies how money works. (Woh ilm jo paisay aur resources ke baray mein parhata hai.)
*   **Economist:** An expert in economics. (Maahir-e-maashiyaat.)
*   **Economic:** About money or trade. (Maashi ya paison se mutaliq.)
*   **Economical:** Not wasting money or time. (Kifayat-shi'aar; jo cheez sasti aur faiday-mand ho.)
*   **Uneconomical:** A waste of money. (Jo cheez mehngi paray ya faida na ho.)
*   **Economically:** In a way that is cheap or efficient. (Sastay tareeqay se ya kifayat se.)

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Economy:** System (Nizam), Market (Baazaar).
*   **Economic:** Financial (Maali), Monetary (Paison se mutaliq).
*   **Economical:** Efficient (Asar-dar/kam kharch), Thrifty (Kifayat-shi'aar).

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Economize:**
    1. We must economize on server space to save costs. (Hamein server space bachanay ke liye kharch kam karna hoga.)
    2. The startup had to economize to survive the year. (Startup ko saal bhar chalnay ke liye kharch kam karna para.)
*   **Economy:**
    1. The digital economy is growing very fast. (Digital maeeshat tezi se barh rahi hai.)
    2. A stable economy helps tech companies grow. (Ek mazboot maeeshat tech companies ko barhnay mein madad karti hai.)
*   **Economics:**
    1. She is studying Economics at the university. (Woh university mein Economics parh rahi hai.)
    2. The economics of cloud computing are very interesting. (Cloud computing ka maashi pehlu bohat dilchasp hai.)
*   **Economist:**
    1. The economist predicted a change in the market. (Maahir-e-maashiyaat ne market mein tabdeeli ki peshan-goi ki.)
    2. Many economists are studying the impact of AI. (Bohat se maahir-e-maashiyaat AI ke asraat parh rahay hain.)
*   **Economic:**
    1. There are many economic benefits of using open-source software. (Open-source software istemaal karne ke bohat se maashi faiday hain.)
    2. The economic growth of the country depends on technology. (Mulq ki maashi taraqqi technology par munhasir hai.)
*   **Economical:**
    1. This algorithm is very economical for small computers. (Yeh algorithm chotay computers ke liye bohat sasta aur behtar hai.)
    2. We need an economical solution for data storage. (Hamein data store karne ke liye ek sasta aur acha hal chahiye.)
*   **Uneconomical:**
    1. Old hardware becomes uneconomical to repair. (Purana hardware theek karwana mehnga aur be-faida ho jata hai.)
    2. Running the server 24/7 is uneconomical for this project. (Is project ke liye server ko har waqt chalana mehnga par raha hai.)
*   **Economically:**
    1. The new software is economically efficient. (Naya software maashi tor par bohat faiday-mand hai.)
    2. We need to plan economically to save the budget. (Hamein budget bachanay ke liye kifayat se plan karna hoga.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The global **economy** is changing due to AI. (AI ki wajah se alami maeeshat badal rahi hai.)
2. It is **economical** to use cloud storage instead of buying servers. (Server khareednay ke bajaye cloud storage istemaal karna sasta parta hai.)
3. **Economists** believe technology increases productivity. (Maahir-e-maashiyaat ka manna hai ke technology paidawar barhati hai.)
4. We must **economize** on electricity consumption in the data center. (Data center mein bijli ka kharch kam karna chahiye.)
5. The **economic** impact of the internet is huge. (Internet ka maashi asar bohat bara hai.)
6. Developing custom software is **uneconomical** for small shops. (Choti dukano ke liye custom software banwana mehnga aur be-faida hai.)
7. The company is **economically** stable. (Company maashi tor par mazboot hai.)
8. **Economics** teaches us how to manage limited resources. (Economics hamein sikhati hai ke mehdood resources ko kaise manage karna hai.)
9. Many **economies** are now moving to digital currencies. (Bohat si maeeshatein ab digital currency ki taraf ja rahi hain.)
10. Using recycled parts is an **economical** choice. (Recycled parts istemaal karna ek sasta aur acha faisla hai.)
11. The project failed because it was **uneconomical**. (Project nakam ho gaya kyunke yeh mehnga aur be-faida tha.)
12. **Economically**, this plan is the best for us. (Maashi tor par, yeh plan hamaray liye behtareen hai.)
13. The professor of **Economics** explained the market trends. (Economics ke professor ne market trends samjhaye.)
14. An **economist** analyzed the software industry data. (Ek maahir-e-maashiyaat ne software industry ke data ka tajziya kiya.)
15. We need to **economize** our time to finish the coding project. (Coding project khatam karne ke liye hamein waqt ki bachat karni hogi.)

**Memory Trick:**
*   **Eco-** usually means "house" or "resources."
*   **Economical:** Ends in "cal," think of "calculation"—if you calculate your money, you become **economical** (saving money).
