Hello! I am your English instructor. Today, we will learn about the word **"Distribute"** and its family. This word is very important in Computer Science (like distributing data or tasks). Let’s learn together!

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:** Distribute, Distributing, Redistribute, Redistributed, Redistributes, Redistributing.
*   **Noun forms:** Distribution, Distributions, Distributor, Distributors, Redistribution.
*   **Adjective forms:** Distributed, Distributional, Distributive.
*   **Adverb forms:** None common.

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Distribute (Verb):** To give or spread things over an area. (Cheezon ko alag alag jagah ya logon mein phelana.)
*   **Distributed (Adjective/Verb):** Spread out. (Jo pheli hui ho, jaise Computer Science mein 'distributed system'.)
*   **Distributing (Verb):** The act of spreading. (Cheezein phelane ka amal.)
*   **Distribution (Noun):** The way things are shared. (Cheezon ko bante ya phelane ka tariqa.)
*   **Distributional (Adjective):** Related to how things are distributed. (Taqseem ya phelane se mutaliq.)
*   **Distributions (Noun - Plural):** Multiple ways of sharing. (Alag alag taqseem ke tariqe.)
*   **Distributive (Adjective):** Related to the act of giving out. (Taqseem karne wali sifat.)
*   **Distributor (Noun):** A person or company that sells/gives things. (Woh jo cheezein aage pohanchata hai.)
*   **Distributors (Noun - Plural):** Many people/companies who share things. (Ek se zyada log jo cheezein aage pohanchate hain.)
*   **Redistribute (Verb):** To share again in a new way. (Dobara taqseem karna.)
*   **Redistributed (Verb):** Shared again. (Jo dobara taqseem ki gayi ho.)
*   **Redistributes (Verb):** Shares again (for he/she/it). (Woh jo dobara taqseem karta hai.)
*   **Redistributing (Verb):** The act of sharing again. (Dobara taqseem karne ka amal.)
*   **Redistribution (Noun):** The act of sharing something again. (Dobara taqseem ka amal.)

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

1.  **Allocate:** To give a share. (Hissa dena.)
2.  **Disseminate:** To spread information. (Information ya baat phelana.)
3.  **Circulate:** To move around. (Ghumana ya phelana.)

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Distribute:** We need to distribute the workload among servers. (Hamein kaam ka bojh servers mein baantna hai.)
*   **Distributed:** This is a distributed computing network. (Yeh ek distributed (pheli hui) computing network hai.)
*   **Distribution:** The distribution of data packets is fast. (Data packets ki taqseem tez hai.)
*   **Distributor:** The software distributor updated the app. (Software distributor ne app update ki.)
*   **Redistribute:** We must redistribute the memory resources. (Hamein memory resources ko dobara taqseem karna hoga.)
*   **Redistribution:** The redistribution of files improved speed. (Files ki dobara taqseem ne speed behtar kar di.)

*(Note: Similar logic applies to other forms like 'Distributing', 'Distributive', etc.)*

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The system will **distribute** the files automatically. (System files ko khud-ba-khud phela dega.)
2. We use a **distributed** database for high traffic. (Hum zyada traffic ke liye distributed database istemal karte hain.)
3. **Distributing** updates takes a long time. (Updates phelane mein waqt lagta hai.)
4. The **distribution** of power is equal. (Power ki taqseem barabar hai.)
5. Check the **distributional** properties of the data. (Data ki taqseem se mutaliq khasiyat check karein.)
6. There are many **distributions** of Linux available. (Linux ki bohat si distributions dastiyab hain.)
7. The **distributive** law is used in algorithms. (Distributive law algorithms mein istemal hota hai.)
8. Contact the official **distributor** for the hardware. (Hardware ke liye official distributor se rabta karein.)
9. Many **distributors** offer cloud services. (Bohat se distributors cloud services dete hain.)
10. The script will **redistribute** the tasks. (Script tasks ko dobara taqseem kar degi.)
11. The load was **redistributed** across the cluster. (Load ko cluster mein dobara taqseem kiya gaya.)
12. The server **redistributes** data every hour. (Server har ghante data dobara taqseem karta hai.)
13. **Redistributing** the load helps the system. (Load ko dobara taqseem karna system ki madad karta hai.)
14. The **redistribution** of resources fixed the bug. (Resources ki dobara taqseem ne bug theek kar diya.)
15. Efficiency depends on the **distribution** method. (Efficiency taqseem ke tariqe par depend karti hai.)
