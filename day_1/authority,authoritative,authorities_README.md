Let’s start!

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Authorize (to give official permission)
    *   Re-authorize (to give permission again - less common)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Authority (the power or right to give orders)
    *   Authorities (people or groups in control)
    *   Authorization (the act of giving permission)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Authoritative (reliable, trusted, and true)
    *   Unauthorized (not having permission)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   Authoritatively (in a way that shows power or knowledge)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Authorize (Verb):** To give official permission.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi kaam ko karne ki official ijazat dena.
*   **Authority (Noun):** The power to make decisions.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi ke paas faisla karne ki taqat ya ikhtiyar hona.
*   **Authorities (Noun):** People who are in charge.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Wo log jin ke paas control hota hai (jaise police ya management).
*   **Authorization (Noun):** The document or process of permission.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Permission ka paper ya process.
*   **Authoritative (Adjective):** Something that is trusted because it comes from an expert.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo cheez bilkul sahi aur expert ki taraf se ho, jis par bharosa kiya ja sake.
*   **Unauthorized (Adjective):** Not allowed or illegal.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jis kaam ki ijazat na ho, ya illegal ho.
*   **Authoritatively (Adverb):** In a confident way.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Poore confidence aur ikhtiyar ke sath.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Authority:**
    *   Power (Taqat)
    *   Control (Control/Ikhtiyar)
    *   Expertise (Maharat)
*   **Authoritative:**
    *   Reliable (Qabil-e-bharosa)
    *   Official (Sarkari/Official)
    *   Expert (Maahir)

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Authorize:** The system will **authorize** your login after checking the password.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* System password check karne ke baad aapke login ko authorize (ijazat) karega.
*   **Authority:** The admin has the **authority** to change database settings.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Admin ke paas database settings change karne ka ikhtiyar hai.
*   **Authorities:** The cyber security **authorities** are investigating the data breach.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Cyber security authorities data breach ki investigation kar rahi hain.
*   **Authorization:** Please provide your **authorization** code to access the server.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Server access karne ke liye apna authorization code dein.
*   **Authoritative:** This textbook is an **authoritative** source on algorithms.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Yeh textbook algorithms par ek authoritative (reliable) source hai.
*   **Unauthorized:** The firewall blocks **unauthorized** access to the network.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Firewall network par unauthorized (baghair ijazat) access ko block karti hai.
*   **Authoritatively:** The professor spoke **authoritatively** about AI ethics.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Professor ne AI ethics ke baare mein poore confidence ke sath baat ki.

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The manager must **authorize** the new software installation. (Manager ko software installation authorize karni chahiye.)
2. Only users with the right **authority** can edit the master file. (Sirf sahi ikhtiyar wale users hi file edit kar sakte hain.)
3. The **authorities** released a report on cyber safety. (Authorities ne cyber safety par report jari ki.)
4. **Unauthorized** users cannot enter the secure zone. (Unauthorized users secure zone mein nahi aa sakte.)
5. This is the most **authoritative** guide on coding standards. (Yeh coding standards par sab se behtareen aur reliable guide hai.)
6. You need **authorization** to run this script. (Is script ko chalane ke liye aapko authorization chahiye.)
7. The lead developer acted **authoritatively** to fix the bug. (Lead developer ne bug fix karne ke liye poore ikhtiyar se kaam kiya.)
8. The system will **authorize** the payment automatically. (System khud hi payment authorize kar dega.)
9. The university **authorities** updated the IT policy. (University ki authorities ne IT policy update ki.)
10. We found **unauthorized** devices on the network. (Humne network par unauthorized devices dhund li hain.)
11. The research paper is an **authoritative** source for our project. (Yeh research paper hamare project ke liye ek reliable source hai.)
12. Did you get **authorization** from the supervisor? (Kya aapne supervisor se authorization li?)
13. The CEO has the **authority** to approve the budget. (CEO ke paas budget approve karne ka ikhtiyar hai.)
14. Always use **authoritative** data for your research. (Apni research ke liye hamesha reliable data use karein.)
15. The system denied the **unauthorized** request. (System ne unauthorized request ko reject kar diya.)
