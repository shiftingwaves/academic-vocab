Hello! I am your English instructor. Today, we will learn a very important academic word family: **Consist**. These words are used a lot in Computer Science and research to describe what something is made of or how things work together.

Let’s start learning!

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:** consist, consists, consisted, consisting
*   **Noun forms:** consistency, inconsistency, inconsistencies
*   **Adjective forms:** consistent, inconsistent
*   **Adverb forms:** consistently

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Consist:** To be made of specific parts. (Kisi cheez ka kin cheezon se mil kar banna.)
*   **Consists / Consisted / Consisting:** These are different tenses of "consist." (Ye 'consist' ki alag alag forms hain.)
*   **Consistency:** When something stays the same and does not change. (Jab koi cheez hamesha ek jaisi rahe, badle nahi.)
*   **Inconsistency:** When things do not match or change too much. (Jab cheezon mein farq ho ya wo barabar na hon.)
*   **Inconsistencies:** More than one example of things not matching. (Ek se zyada jagah jahan cheezein barabar na hon.)
*   **Consistent:** Always acting or behaving in the same way. (Jo cheez hamesha ek jaisi rahe, bharose-mand.)
*   **Inconsistent:** Not staying the same; changing in a way that is confusing. (Jo cheez barabar na ho ya baar-baar badle.)
*   **Consistently:** Doing something in the same good way every time. (Hamesha ek hi tarah se kaam karna.)

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Consist:** The computer system consists of hardware and software. (Computer system hardware aur software se mil kar banta hai.)
*   **Consists:** A database consists of many tables. (Database bohot si tables par mushtamil hota hai.)
*   **Consisted:** The research project consisted of three main stages. (Research project teen marhalon par mushtamil tha.)
*   **Consisting:** The code, consisting of 100 lines, works well. (Ye code, jo 100 lines par mushtamil hai, sahi kaam karta hai.)
*   **Consistency:** We need consistency in our coding style. (Hamein apne coding style mein ek jaisa pan chahiye.)
*   **Inconsistency:** There is an inconsistency in the data values. (Data ki values mein farq/gadbad hai.)
*   **Inconsistencies:** The report shows many inconsistencies. (Report mein bohot si galtiyan/farq hain.)
*   **Consistent:** The results were consistent with our theory. (Results hamari theory ke mutabiq/barabar thay.)
*   **Inconsistent:** The output was inconsistent during the test. (Test ke dauran output barabar nahi aa raha tha.)
*   **Consistently:** The program performs consistently every time. (Program har baar ek hi tarah se achha kaam karta hai.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1.  The network consists of several servers. (Network kai servers par mushtamil hai.)
2.  Data consistency is important for security. (Security ke liye data ka ek jaisa hona zaroori hai.)
3.  The team worked consistently to finish the project. (Team ne project khatam karne ke liye lagatar mehnat ki.)
4.  His logic was inconsistent with the facts. (Us ki logic haqaiq ke khilaf/alag thi.)
5.  Small inconsistencies can cause big bugs. (Chhoti chhoti galtiyan bade bugs paida kar sakti hain.)
6.  The file consists of user profiles. (Ye file user profiles par mushtamil hai.)
7.  We need a consistent approach to solve this. (Hamein ise hal karne ke liye ek jaisa tareeqa chahiye.)
8.  The algorithm consisted of simple steps. (Algorithm aasaan steps par mushtamil tha.)
9.  Avoid inconsistency in your variable naming. (Variables ke naam rakhne mein farq na rakhein.)
10. The system is consistently fast. (System hamesha ek jaisa fast hai.)
11. Are these results consistent? (Kya ye results ek jaise hain?)
12. The app consists of a clean interface. (App ek saaf-suthre interface par mushtamil hai.)
13. Any inconsistency will stop the process. (Koi bhi farq/gadbad process ko rok degi.)
14. They consistently used the same library. (Unhon ne hamesha ek hi library ka istemal kiya.)
15. The findings are inconsistent with earlier reports. (Ye findings pehli reports se alag hain.)
