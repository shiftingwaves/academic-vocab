Hello! I am your English language instructor. Today, we will learn about the word family **"Environment"**. This is a very important word in academic writing and Computer Science (like "cloud environment" or "development environment").

Let’s start!

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   (None commonly used)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Environment (the place/surroundings)
    *   Environments (plural form)
    *   Environmentalist (a person who cares about nature)
    *   Environmentalists (plural form)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Environmental (related to the surroundings)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   Environmentally (in a way that relates to the surroundings)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Environment:** The conditions or surroundings where something exists.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh jagah ya halaat jahan aap kaam karte hain ya rehte hain.
*   **Environments:** Plural of environment.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada jagah ya halaat.
*   **Environmentalist:** A person who works to protect nature.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh shakhs jo qudrat aur mahol ki hifazat ke liye kaam karta hai.
*   **Environmentalists:** Plural of environmentalist.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada log jo mahol ki hifazat karte hain.
*   **Environmental:** About the surroundings or nature.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Mahol ya qudrat se talluq rakhne wala.
*   **Environmentally:** In a way that affects or relates to the environment.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Mahol ke hawale se ya mahol ke zariye.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Environment:**
    *   Surroundings (aas-paas ki cheezein)
    *   Setting (ek khaas jagah ya halaat)
    *   Context (mahol ya pas-e-manzar)
*   **Environmental:**
    *   Ecological (qudrati mahol se talluq rakhne wala)

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Environment:**
    *   The software runs best in a Linux **environment**. (Software Linux ke mahol mein behtar chalta hai.)
    *   A stable **environment** is needed for research. (Research ke liye ek mustahkam mahol ki zaroorat hoti hai.)
*   **Environments:**
    *   Developers test code in different **environments**. (Developers code ko mukhtalif mahol mein test karte hain.)
    *   Cloud **environments** help store big data. (Cloud environments big data store karne mein madad karte hain.)
*   **Environmentalist:**
    *   The **environmentalist** spoke about climate change. (Environmentalist ne climate change ke baare mein baat ki.)
*   **Environmentalists:**
    *   Many **environmentalists** study the impact of technology. (Bahut se environmentalists technology ke asar ka mutala karte hain.)
*   **Environmental:**
    *   **Environmental** factors affect server performance. (Maholiyati awamil server ki karkardagi par asar daalte hain.)
    *   We must consider the **environmental** impact of e-waste. (Hamein e-waste ke maholiyati asraat par gaur karna chahiye.)
*   **Environmentally:**
    *   We need **environmentally** friendly computers. (Hamein mahol dost computers ki zaroorat hai.)
    *   The lab is **environmentally** controlled. (Lab ka mahol control kiya gaya hai.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1.  The coding **environment** must be set up properly. (Coding ka mahol theek se set hona chahiye.)
2.  **Environmentalists** are worried about electronic waste. (Environmentalists electronic kachre ko lekar pareshan hain.)
3.  Our **environmental** policy is very strict. (Hamari maholiyati policy bahut sakht hai.)
4.  Testing in various **environments** reduces bugs. (Mukhtalif mahol mein testing karne se bugs kam hote hain.)
5.  This server is **environmentally** safe. (Yeh server mahol ke liye mehfooz hai.)
6.  The **environment** of the lab is very quiet. (Lab ka mahol bahut khamosh hai.)
7.  Modern **environmentalists** use data to save trees. (Jadeed environmentalists darakhton ko bachane ke liye data ka istemal karte hain.)
8.  Choose an **environmentally** sustainable laptop. (Aisa laptop chune jo mahol ke liye nuksan-deh na ho.)
9.  The **environmental** impact of data centers is huge. (Data centers ka maholiyati asar bahut zyada hai.)
10. Does your IDE provide a good **environment**? (Kya aapka IDE ek acha mahol deta hai?)
11. Many **environmentalists** suggest recycling hardware. (Bahut se environmentalists hardware ko recycle karne ka mashwara dete hain.)
12. The **environmental** conditions are perfect for the server. (Maholiyati halaat server ke liye behtareen hain.)
13. Creating multiple **environments** helps in deployment. (Kai environments banana deployment mein madad karta hai.)
14. An **environmentally** conscious company saves energy. (Mahol ka khayal rakhne wali company bijli bachati hai.)
15. The research **environment** encourages new ideas. (Research ka mahol naye khayalat ki hosla afzai karta hai.)

| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Environment** | The setting or place | Software development / Research lab |
| **Environmental** | Related to nature/surroundings | Analyzing impact (e.g., e-waste) |

| **Environmentally** | In a nature-friendly way | Describing green technology |

**Memory Trick:**
*   **Enviro-** sounds like "in-very-near". Think of your **environment** as everything that is "in-very-near" to you! 
*   **-ist** always means a "person" (like a Scientist or Artist). So, an **Environmentalist** is a person for the environment.
