### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:** contract, contracted, contracting, contracts
*   **Noun forms:** contract, contractor, contractors
*   **Adjective forms:** contractual (related to a contract)
*   **Adverb forms:** contractually (related to a contract)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Contract (Verb):** To make an agreement or to get smaller. 
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi kaam ke liye legal agreement karna, ya kisi cheez ka size mein chota ho jana.
*   **Contracted (Verb/Adj):** Something that has been agreed upon or made smaller.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo kaam agree ho chuka ho, ya jo size mein chota ho gaya ho.
*   **Contracting (Verb):** The action of making an agreement or shrinking.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Agreement kar rahe hona, ya chota ho rahe hona.
*   **Contracts (Verb/Noun):** Plural of agreement or the action of shrinking.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada legal papers ya agreements.
*   **Contractor (Noun):** A person or company hired to do a specific job.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh banda ya company jo kisi project ko complete karne ka theka (contract) leti hai.
*   **Contractors (Noun):** Plural of contractor.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada log ya companies jo project ka kaam karte hain.

### 3. SYNONYMS
