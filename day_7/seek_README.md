Hello! I am your English instructor. Today, we are going to learn the word **"Seek"**. This is a very important academic word. In Computer Science, we "seek" (search for) information, data, or solutions.

Let’s start our lesson!

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Seek (Base form)
    *   Seeks (Third-person singular)
    *   Seeking (Present participle/Gerund)
    *   Sought (Past tense and Past participle)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Seeker (A person who seeks)
    *   Seeking (The act of looking for something)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   None (Commonly used)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   None

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Seek:** To look for something or try to find something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ko talash karna ya hasil karne ki koshish karna.
*   **Seeks:** He/She/It looks for something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh talash karta hai (jab hum kisi aik cheez ya person ki baat karte hain).
*   **Seeking:** Looking for something right now.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Talash kar raha hai ya dhond raha hai.
*   **Sought:** Looked for something in the past.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Talash kiya tha ya dhonda tha.
*   **Seeker:** A person who is looking for something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Talash karne wala.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

1.  **Search:** Talash karna.
2.  **Look for:** Kisi cheez ki koshish mein hona.
3.  **Pursue:** Peecha karna ya hasil karne ki koshish karna.

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Seek:** Researchers often **seek** new ways to improve data security.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Researchers aksar data security ko behtar banane ke naye tareeqay talash karte hain.
    *   The program will **seek** the missing file in the folder.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Program folder mein missing file ko talash karega.

*   **Seeks:** The software **seeks** to reduce errors in coding.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Yeh software coding mein ghaltiyon ko kam karne ki koshish karta hai.
    *   The system **seeks** user permission before accessing the camera.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* System camera access karne se pehle user ki ijazat mangta hai.

*   **Seeking:** Many students are **seeking** better methods to learn Python.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Bohat se students Python seekhne ke behtar tareeqay talash kar rahe hain.
    *   The company is currently **seeking** expert developers.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Company filhal mahir developers ko talash kar rahi hai.

*   **Sought:** The engineers **sought** a solution to the network problem.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Engineers ne network problem ka hal talash kiya tha.
    *   The team **sought** advice from their professor about the research paper.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Team ne apne professor se research paper ke bare mein mashwara liya tha.

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. We **seek** to optimize the algorithm. (Hum algorithm ko behtar banane ki koshish karte hain.)
2. He is **seeking** a job in AI. (Woh AI mein naukri talash kar raha hai.)
3. They **sought** help from the database expert. (Unhon ne database expert se madad li thi.)
4. The system **seeks** faster processing speeds. (System tez processing speeds talash karta hai.)
5. Are you **seeking** more information about cloud computing? (Kya aap cloud computing ke bare mein mazeed maloomat dhoond rahe hain?)
6. The scientist **sought** evidence for his theory. (Scientist ne apni theory ke liye saboot talash kiya tha.)
7. Our goal is to **seek** innovation. (Hamara maqsad innovation talash karna hai.)
8. The program **seeks** errors in the script. (Program script mein ghaltiyan dhoondta hai.)
9. Many users are **seeking** secure apps. (Bohat se users mehfooz apps talash kar rahe hain.)
10. The researchers **sought** new data sources. (Researchers ne naye data sources talash kiye the.)
11. She **seeks** to understand machine learning. (Woh machine learning ko samajhne ki koshish karti hai.)
12. The startup is **seeking** investment. (Startup investment talash kar raha hai.)
13. They **sought** a bug in the code. (Unhon ne code mein bug talash kiya tha.)
14. The AI **seeks** patterns in big data. (AI big data mein patterns dhoondta hai.)
15. We have **sought** better ways to store files. (Hum ne files store karne ke behtar tareeqay dhoonde hain.)

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

**Core Meaning:** To try to find or achieve something.

| Word | Simple Meaning | CS/Academic Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Seek** | To look for | Searching for data/solutions |
| **Seeks** | Looks for (Singular) | The system searches |
| **Seeking** | Looking for (Ongoing) | Active research/Development |
| **Sought** | Looked for (Past) | Past research/Experiments |

**Memory Trick:** Think of **"Seek"** like **"Search"**. They both start with **"S"**. When you are a **"Seeker"**, you are on a **"Search"** mission!
