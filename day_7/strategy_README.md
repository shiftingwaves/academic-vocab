Hello! I am so happy to help you learn these words. Today, we will look at the word **"Strategy"**. This is a very important word in Computer Science and research. Think of a "strategy" as a "smart plan" to win or finish a hard job.

Let’s start our lesson!

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Strategize (to make a plan)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Strategy (the plan)
    *   Strategies (plural of strategy - many plans)
    *   Strategist (a person who makes plans)
    *   Strategists (plural of strategist - many people who make plans)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Strategic (related to a plan)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   Strategically (in a way that uses a plan)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Strategize:** To think and create a plan.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Soch samajh kar koi plan banana.
*   **Strategy:** A careful plan to reach a goal.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek khaas plan ya tareeqa jo kisi maqsad ko hasil karne ke liye banaya jaye.
*   **Strategies:** More than one plan.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada plans.
*   **Strategist:** A person who is good at making plans.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh shakhs jo plan banane mein mahir ho.
*   **Strategists:** Many people who make plans.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Bohat saare plan banane wale log.
*   **Strategic:** Something that is very important and part of a big plan.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo cheez kisi bade plan ka hissa ho ya bohat ahem ho.
*   **Strategically:** Doing something in a smart, planned way.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi kaam ko soch samajh kar aur plan ke mutabiq karna.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Strategy:** 
    1.  **Plan:** (Ek tareeqa/mansooba)

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Strategize:**
    1. Developers must strategize before writing complex code. (Developers ko mushkil code likhne se pehle plan banana chahiye.)
    2. The team met to strategize the project timeline. (Team ne project ki timeline ka plan banane ke liye meeting ki.)
*   **Strategy:**
    1. This new algorithm is a good strategy for data sorting. (Yeh naya algorithm data sort karne ke liye ek acha plan hai.)
    2. We need a clear strategy to prevent cyber attacks. (Hamein cyber attacks rokne ke liye ek saaf plan ki zaroorat hai.)
*   **Strategies:**
    1. There are many strategies to improve network speed. (Network ki speed behtar karne ke liye bohat se plans/tareeqay hain.)
    2. The researchers tested different security strategies. (Researchers ne security ke mukhtalif tareeqon ko test kiya.)
*   **Strategist:**
    1. The lead software strategist designed the system architecture. (Lead software strategist ne system ka design banaya.)
    2. A good strategist thinks about future errors. (Ek acha plan banane wala hamesha aane wali ghaltiyon ke baare mein sochta hai.)
*   **Strategists:**
    1. Many strategists work in big tech companies. (Bohat se plan banane wale bade tech companies mein kaam karte hain.)
    2. The strategists suggested a new way to store data. (In logon ne data save karne ka naya tareeqa bataya.)
*   **Strategic:**
    1. This is a strategic move to help the company grow. (Yeh company ko barhane ke liye ek ahem aur soch-samajh kar liya gaya qadam hai.)
    2. We made a strategic choice to use Python. (Humne Python use karne ka ek soch-samajh kar faisla kiya.)
*   **Strategically:**
    1. The sensors were placed strategically around the room. (Sensors ko kamre mein soch-samajh kar sahi jagah lagaya gaya.)
    2. We strategically moved our data to the cloud. (Humne soch-samajh kar apna data cloud par shift kiya.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The IT team needs to **strategize** for the upcoming update. (IT team ko agle update ke liye plan banana chahiye.)
2. A winning **strategy** is important for software success. (Software ki kamyabi ke liye ek acha plan zaroori hai.)
3. We compared different **strategies** for database management. (Humne database management ke liye mukhtalif tareeqon ka mawazna kiya.)
4. The lead **strategist** reviewed the network security. (Lead strategist ne network security ka jaiza liya.)
5. Many **strategists** believe AI will change the world. (Bohat se mahireen ka khayal hai ke AI duniya badal dega.)
6. This **strategic** partnership will help our research. (Yeh ahem partnership hamari research mein madad karegi.)
7. We **strategically** placed the servers to keep them cool. (Humne servers ko soch-samajh kar thandi jagah par rakha.)
8. To **strategize** effectively, you need good data. (Sahi se plan banane ke liye aapko achay data ki zaroorat hoti hai.)
9. This **strategy** reduces the risk of system failure. (Yeh plan system failure ka khatra kam karta hai.)
10. The company uses many **strategies** to stop hackers. (Company hackers ko rokne ke liye bohat se tareeqay use karti hai.)
11. He is a famous **strategist** in the gaming industry. (Woh gaming industry mein ek mashhoor plan banane wala hai.)
12. The **strategists** agreed on the new system design. (Sabhi logon ne naye system design par ittefaq kiya.)
13. Making a **strategic** plan saves time. (Soch-samajh kar plan banana waqt bachata hai.)
14. The data was **strategically** organized for fast access. (Data ko jaldi access karne ke liye sahi tareeqe se rakha gaya.)
15. You must **strategize** your study time for exams. (Aapko exams ke liye apne parhai ke waqt ka plan banana chahiye.)
