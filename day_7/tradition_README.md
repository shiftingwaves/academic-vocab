---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Tradition (Noun):** A belief or custom passed down for a long time.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Purani rasmein ya tareeqay jo log lambay arsay se follow kar rahay hon.
*   **Traditions (Noun):** More than one tradition.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada purani rasmein.
*   **Traditionalist (Noun):** A person who likes old ways.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh shakhs jo purani riwayaton ko pasand karta ho.
*   **Traditional (Adjective):** Something that follows old ways.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo puranay tareeqon ke mutabiq ho.
*   **Non-traditional (Adjective):** Something new or different from old ways.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo puranay tareeqon se hat kar ho, naya tareeqa.
*   **Traditionally (Adverb):** In a way that has been done for a long time.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Riwayati taur par, jaisa hamesha se hota aaya hai.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Tradition:**
    *   Custom (Rasm/Riwayat)
    *   Practice (Amal/Tareeqa)
*   **Traditional:**
    *   Conventional (Riwayati/Aam)
    *   Standard (Mayari/Purana)

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Tradition:**
    *   Computer science follows the **tradition** of documenting code. (CS mein code ko document karna ek purani riwayat hai.)
    *   Academic **tradition** requires citing sources correctly. (Taleemi riwayat ke mutabiq sources ka hawala dena zaroori hai.)
*   **Traditions:**
    *   Old **traditions** in software testing are changing. (Software testing mein purani rasmein badal rahi hain.)
    *   Universities keep many academic **traditions** alive. (Universities bohat si taleemi riwayaton ko zinda rakhti hain.)
*   **Traditionalist:**
    *   The **traditionalist** professor prefers books over digital files. (Riwayati soch rakhne wala professor digital files ke bajaye kitabon ko tarjeeh deta hai.)
    *   He is a **traditionalist** who avoids using AI tools. (Woh ek riwayati shakhs hai jo AI tools use karne se gurez karta hai.)
*   **Traditional:**
    *   We used **traditional** methods to solve the algorithm. (Hum ne algorithm solve karne ke liye riwayati tareeqay istemal kiye.)
    *   The **traditional** classroom is moving to online platforms. (Riwayati classroom ab online platforms par muntakil ho raha hai.)
*   **Non-traditional:**
    *   She chose a **non-traditional** way to learn coding. (Us ne coding seekhne ke liye ek naya aur hat kar tareeqa chuna.)
    *   **Non-traditional** students often study at night. (Woh students jo aam riwayat se hat kar hain, aksar raat ko parhte hain.)
*   **Traditionally:**
    *   **Traditionally**, data was stored in local drives. (Riwayati taur par, data local drives mein store kiya jata tha.)
    *   Physics was **traditionally** taught using blackboards. (Physics riwayati taur par blackboards ka istemal karke parhayi jati thi.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The **tradition** of peer review is vital for research. (Research ke liye peer review ki riwayat bohot zaroori hai.)
2. Many **traditional** programming languages are still popular. (Bohat si riwayati programming languages ab bhi mashhoor hain.)
3. We are moving away from **traditional** hardware setups. (Hum riwayati hardware setups se door ja rahe hain.)
4. **Traditionally**, computer scientists worked in offices. (Riwayati taur par, computer scientists offices mein kaam karte thay.)
5. This is a **non-traditional** approach to network security. (Yeh network security ke liye ek naya aur hat kar tareeqa hai.)
6. A **traditionalist** might dislike using cloud storage. (Ek riwayati soch ka banda shayad cloud storage pasand na kare.)
7. Following established **traditions** helps keep data organized. (Qaim shuda riwayaton ko follow karne se data ko organize rakhne mein madad milti hai.)
8. The **traditional** way of coding is becoming faster. (Coding ka riwayati tareeqa ab tez ho raha hai.)
9. Many students prefer **non-traditional** learning schedules. (Bohat se students naye aur hat kar parhai ke schedules pasand karte hain.)
10. **Traditionally**, research papers are written in formal English. (Riwayati taur par, research papers formal English mein likhay jatay hain.)
11. He respects the **tradition** of academic honesty. (Woh taleemi imandari ki riwayat ka ehtram karta hai.)
12. **Non-traditional** methods can lead to new discoveries. (Hat kar kiye gaye tareeqay nayi daryafton ka sabab ban sakte hain.)
13. Some professors are **traditionalists** about handwriting. (Kuch professors handwriting ke mamlay mein riwayati soch rakhte hain.)
14. The **traditional** model of education is evolving. (Taleem ka riwayati model badal raha hai.)
15. We must respect the **traditions** of scientific research. (Hamein scientific research ki riwayaton ka ehtram karna chahiye.)

**Memory Trick:** Think of **"Tradition"** as **"T-R-A-D-I"** (Time-Related-Always-Done-In-past). If it was done in the past for a long time, it is a tradition!
