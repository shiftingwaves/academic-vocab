### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Reside (Verb):** To live in a place for a long time. 
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi jagah par rehna ya qayam pazeer hona.
*   **Resides (Verb):** He/She/It lives in a place.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh rehta hai (jab subject singular ho).
*   **Residing (Verb):** Living in a place right now.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Reh raha hona.
*   **Resided (Verb):** Lived in a place in the past.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Rehta tha ya qayam kiya tha.
*   **Residence (Noun):** A place where someone lives (a house or home).
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Rehayish ki jagah ya ghar.
*   **Resident (Noun):** A person who lives in a specific place.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Rehne wala shakhs (bashinda).
*   **Residents (Noun):** People who live in a specific place (plural).
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Rehne wale log.
*   **Residential (Adjective):** Related to areas where people live, not offices or factories.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Rehayishi ilaqon se mutaliq.

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Reside:**

    2.  **Live:** Rehna.
*   **Residence:**
    1.  **Dwelling:** Rehayish gah.
    2.  **Home:** Ghar.

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Reside:**
    1. Many international students reside on campus. (Bohat se international students campus par rehte hain.)
    2. Data files reside in the main server. (Data files main server mein hoti hain.)
*   **Resides:**
    1. The software resides on the cloud. (Software cloud par hota hai.)
    2. The main database resides in a secure location. (Main database aik mehfooz jagah par hota hai.)
*   **Residing:**
    1. Researchers residing in the city have better access to the library. (Shehar mein rehne wale researchers ki library tak rasai behtar hai.)
    2. The code is residing in the local folder. (Code local folder mein majood hai.)
*   **Resided:**
    1. The team resided in the lab during the project. (Team project ke doran lab mein rahi.)
    2. He resided in the university hostel for two years. (Woh do saal university hostel mein raha.)
*   **Residence:**
    1. Proof of residence is required for the application. (Application ke liye rehayish ka saboot zaroori hai.)
    2. The professor has a private residence near the university. (Professor ka university ke paas apna ghar hai.)
*   **Resident:**
    1. Every resident of the digital network must have a password. (Digital network ke har user/rehne wale ke paas password hona chahiye.)
    2. A resident expert helped with the technical issues. (Aik wahan rehne wale expert ne technical masail mein madad ki.)
*   **Residents:**
    1. The system sends alerts to all residents of the database. (System database ke tamam users ko alerts bhejta hai.)
    2. Many residents use the local Wi-Fi network. (Bohat se rehne wale local Wi-Fi network use karte hain.)
*   **Residential:**
    1. The project focuses on residential internet connectivity. (Project ka focus gharon ki internet connectivity par hai.)
    2. This area is strictly for residential use. (Yeh area sirf rehayishi istemaal ke liye hai.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The data resides in the hidden directory. (Data chupi hui directory mein majood hai.)
2. Many students prefer residing near the college. (Bohat se students college ke paas rehna pasand karte hain.)
3. He resided in London while completing his PhD. (PhD mukammal karte waqt woh London mein raha.)
4. The residence of the lead researcher is very far. (Lead researcher ka ghar bohat door hai.)
5. Every resident must follow the lab rules. (Har rehne wale ko lab ke rules follow karne honge.)
6. The residents of this building have high-speed internet. (Is building mein rehne walon ke paas high-speed internet hai.)
7. We need to check the residential address for the delivery. (Hamein delivery ke liye rehayishi address check karna hai.)
8. The program resides on the primary hard drive. (Program primary hard drive mein hota hai.)
9. She is currently residing in the university dorm. (Woh filhal university dorm mein reh rahi hai.)
10. The research team resided in the facility for a month. (Research team aik mahine tak facility mein rahi.)
11. Proof of residence is mandatory for the registration. (Registration ke liye rehayish ka saboot lazmi hai.)
12. Each resident has a unique ID number. (Har rehne wale ke paas aik unique ID number hai.)
13. These residents are part of the local community. (Yeh rehne wale local community ka hissa hain.)
14. The company builds residential houses with smart technology. (Company smart technology wale rehayishi ghar banati hai.)
15. Does the file reside in the root folder? (Kya file root folder mein majood hai?)

**Memory Trick:** Think of **"Reside"** as **"Re-side"**. If you **re-side** (stay on the side of) a place, you are living there!
