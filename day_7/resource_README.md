### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Resource
    *   Resourcing
    *   Resourced
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Resource
    *   Resources
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Resourceful
    *   Under-resourced
    *   Unresourceful (less common)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Resource (Noun):** Something you use to finish a task (like money, time, or computers).
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Koi bhi cheez jo kaam mukammal karne mein madad de, jaise computer, paise ya waqt.
*   **Resources (Noun/Plural):** More than one thing used to finish a task.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada cheezein jo kaam mein kaam aayen.
*   **Resource (Verb):** To give or provide things needed for a project.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi kaam ke liye zaroori cheezein ya paise faraham karna.
*   **Resourcing (Verb/Gerund):** The act of providing resources.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Zaroori cheezein dene ka amal.
*   **Resourced (Verb/Past):** Provided with necessary things.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh kaam jise zaroori cheezein de di gayi hon.
*   **Resourceful (Adjective):** Good at finding ways to solve problems.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh insaan jo mushkil mein naye raste dhoond le.
*   **Under-resourced (Adjective):** Not having enough money, tools, or staff.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jab kisi kaam ke liye zaroori cheezein ya paise kam hon.
*   **Unresourceful (Adjective):** Not good at solving problems.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh jo mushkil waqt mein koi hal na nikal sake.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Resource (Noun):**
    *   *Asset:* Koi qeemti cheez.
    *   *Supply:* Zaroori saman jo available ho.
*   **Resourceful (Adjective):**
    *   *Capable:* Kaam karne ke qabil.
    *   *Creative:* Nayi soch rakhne wala.

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Resource:**
    1. The CPU is a limited **resource** in this system. (Is system mein CPU ek mehdood resource hai.)
    2. We need a new **resource** to complete the coding task. (Hamein coding task mukammal karne ke liye naye resource ki zaroorat hai.)
*   **Resources:**
    1. This server shares **resources** with other computers. (Yeh server baqi computers ke sath resources share karta hai.)
    2. Online **resources** helped me learn Python. (Online resources ne mujhe Python seekhne mein madad ki.)
*   **Resource (Verb):**
    1. We must **resource** this project with more developers. (Hamein is project mein aur developers shamil karne honge.)
    2. The company plans to **resource** the new lab. (Company naye lab mein saman dene ka irada rakhti hai.)
*   **Resourcing:**
    1. Better **resourcing** will improve the system speed. (Behtar resourcing se system ki speed behtar hogi.)
    2. The **resourcing** of the IT department is complete. (IT department ko saman dene ka amal mukammal ho gaya hai.)
*   **Resourced:**
    1. The project was well **resourced** by the team. (Project ko team ne achi tarah se resources diye the.)
    2. We are fully **resourced** for the exam. (Hum exam ke liye poori tarah tayyar hain.)
*   **Resourceful:**
    1. A **resourceful** programmer solves bugs quickly. (Ek resourceful programmer jaldi bugs solve kar leta hai.)
    2. She is a **resourceful** student in research. (Woh research mein bahut resourceful student hai.)
*   **Under-resourced:**
    1. The school lab is **under-resourced**. (School ki lab mein saman ki kami hai.)
    2. **Under-resourced** projects often fail. (Jin projects mein saman kam ho, woh aksar fail ho jate hain.)
*   **Unresourceful:**
    1. An **unresourceful** person gives up easily. (Ek unresourceful insaan jaldi haar maan leta hai.)
    2. The approach was **unresourceful** and slow. (Yeh tarika sust aur be-asar tha.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The computer lab is **under-resourced** and needs more computers. (Computer lab mein saman ki kami hai aur mazeed computers chahiye.)
2. A **resourceful** researcher finds data in many places. (Ek resourceful researcher bahut si jaghon se data dhoond leta hai.)
3. We need to **resource** our team with better laptops. (Hamein apni team ko behtar laptops dene honge.)
4. The project **resources** include books and software. (Project ke resources mein kitabein aur software shamil hain.)
5. Lack of **resourcing** caused the project delay. (Resources ki kami ki wajah se project late ho gaya.)
6. He is a **resourceful** coder who fixes errors fast. (Woh ek resourceful coder hai jo jaldi ghaltiyan theek kar leta hai.)
7. The **resourced** lab is now ready for students. (Saman se bhari hui lab ab students ke liye tayyar hai.)
8. Please check the online **resources** for the assignment. (Barah-e-karam assignment ke liye online resources check karein.)
9. Many schools remain **under-resourced** in rural areas. (Dehati ilaqon mein bahut se schools mein abhi bhi resources ki kami hai.)
10. Being **unresourceful** is a barrier to success. (Mushkil mein hal na dhoond pana kamyabi mein rukawat hai.)
11. The team was well **resourced** for the research task. (Team ke paas research ke liye sab zaroori saman tha.)
12. Proper **resourcing** is the key to a good paper. (Behtar resources dena ek achi research paper ki kunji hai.)
13. Every student needs access to learning **resources**. (Har student ko seekhne ke resources tak rasai honi chahiye.)
14. She is **resourceful** enough to manage the server alone. (Woh itni resourceful hai ke akele server manage kar sakti hai.)
15. The system is **under-resourced**, causing it to crash often. (System mein resources kam hain, isliye woh aksar band ho jata hai.)

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

| Word | Simple Meaning | CS / Academic Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Resource** | Zaroori cheez | Hardware, data, or time. |
| **Resources** | Ek se zyada cheezein | Server memory, staff, budget. |
| **Resourceful** | Hal dhoondne wala | Solving coding bugs or research problems. |
| **Under-resourced** | Saman ki kami | Not enough servers or tools. |

**Memory Trick:**
*   **Resource** = **Re** (again) + **Source** (origin). Think of it as the "source" you go back to when you need help or tools to finish your work!
