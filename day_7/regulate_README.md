### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:** regulate, regulates, regulated, regulating, deregulate, deregulates, deregulated, deregulating
*   **Noun forms:** regulation, regulations, regulator, regulators, deregulation
*   **Adjective forms:** regulatory, unregulated
*   **Adverb forms:** (None commonly used)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Regulate (verb):** To control or make rules for something. (Kisi cheez ko control karna ya uske liye rules banana.)
*   **Regulates (verb):** The third-person form of regulate. (Jab hum kisi single cheez ki baat karte hain.)
*   **Regulated (verb/adj):** Controlled by rules. (Wo cheez jise rules ke zariye control kiya gaya ho.)
*   **Regulating (verb):** The act of controlling. (Rules bana kar control karne ka amal.)
*   **Deregulate (verb):** To remove rules. (Rules khatam kar dena ya chhoot de dena.)
*   **Deregulates (verb):** The third-person form of deregulate. (Rules khatam karna.)
*   **Deregulated (verb/adj):** Something that no longer has strict rules. (Wo cheez jis se pabandi hata li gayi ho.)
*   **Deregulating (verb):** The act of removing rules. (Rules hatane ka amal.)
*   **Regulation (noun):** A rule made by an authority. (Ek official rule ya qanoon.)
*   **Regulations (noun):** Plural of regulation. (Bahut saare rules.)
*   **Regulator (noun):** A person or organization that checks rules. (Wo idara jo check karta hai ke sab rules follow kar rahe hain ya nahi.)
*   **Regulators (noun):** Plural of regulator. (Bahut saare check karne wale idaray.)
*   **Regulatory (adjective):** Related to rules. (Rules se talluq rakhne wala.)
*   **Unregulated (adjective):** Not controlled by rules. (Wo cheez jis par koi pabandi ya rule na ho.)

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Regulate:** Control (control karna), Manage (intizam sambhalna).
*   **Regulation:** Rule (qanoon), Law (qanoon).
*   **Regulatory:** Official (sarkari/idaray ka), Administrative (intizami).

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Regulate:** The government must regulate AI to keep data safe. (Hukoomat ko data safe rakhne ke liye AI ko regulate karna chahiye.)
*   **Regulates:** The software regulates how much memory the computer uses. (Ye software control karta hai ke computer kitni memory use kare.)
*   **Regulated:** This sector is highly regulated by the government. (Ye sector hukoomat ke sakht rules ke under hai.)
*   **Regulating:** Regulating online privacy is a difficult task. (Online privacy ko control karna ek mushkil kaam hai.)
*   **Deregulate:** Some companies want to deregulate the internet. (Kuch companies chahti hain ke internet se rules hata diye jayen.)
*   **Deregulates:** The new policy deregulates small tech startups. (Nayi policy choti tech companies se rules hata deti hai.)
*   **Deregulated:** The market became deregulated last year. (Pichle saal market se rules hata diye gaye.)
*   **Deregulating:** Deregulating the market helps some businesses grow. (Market se rules hatane se kuch businesses ko barhne mein madad milti hai.)
*   **Regulation:** New regulation for AI was passed yesterday. (Kal AI ke liye naya qanoon pass hua.)
*   **Regulations:** We must follow all security regulations. (Hamein tamam security rules follow karne chahiye.)
*   **Regulator:** The regulator fined the company for data leaks. (Regulator ne data leak hone par company ko jurmana kiya.)
*   **Regulators:** Regulators are meeting to discuss privacy. (Regulators privacy par baat karne ke liye mil rahe hain.)
*   **Regulatory:** The project needs regulatory approval. (Project ko official ijazat ki zaroorat hai.)
*   **Unregulated:** Unregulated AI can be dangerous. (Jo AI control mein na ho wo khatarnak ho sakta hai.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The government plans to **regulate** Big Tech companies. (Hukoomat Big Tech companies ko regulate karne ka plan bana rahi hai.)
2. New **regulations** protect user data. (Naye rules user ke data ko protect karte hain.)
3. The **regulator** checked the software code. (Regulator ne software code check kiya.)
4. Some experts fear **unregulated** algorithms. (Kuch experts ko control na kiye gaye algorithms se darr lagta hai.)
5. **Regulatory** bodies monitor internet traffic. (Regulatory idaray internet traffic par nazar rakhte hain.)
6. The company **regulates** access to its servers. (Company apne servers tak access ko control karti hai.)
7. **Deregulating** the industry may increase competition. (Industry se rules hatane se shayad competition barh jaye.)
8. **Regulators** have issued a warning to the firm. (Regulators ne firm ko warning di hai.)
9. Many **regulations** are outdated for modern AI. (Bahut se rules modern AI ke liye purane ho chuke hain.)
10. The **regulated** environment ensures stability. (Control kiya gaya mahol stability yaqeeni banata hai.)
11. **Deregulated** markets often change quickly. (Jin markets mein rules nahi hote, wo jaldi badalti hain.)
12. The team is **regulating** the power consumption of the chip. (Team chip ki bijli ki khapat ko control kar rahi hai.)
13. **Regulatory** changes will affect the software design. (Rules mein tabdeeli software design ko mutasir karegi.)
14. It is an **unregulated** space for hackers. (Ye hackers ke liye ek aisi jagah hai jahan koi rule nahi.)
15. Strict **regulations** help prevent cyber attacks. (Sakht rules cyber attacks rokne mein madad karte hain.)

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

**Core Meaning:** Controlling or managing systems through rules.

| Word | Meaning | CS Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Regulate | Control karna | AI ya Software control karna |
| Regulation | Rule/Qanoon | Cyber law ya Data privacy rule |
| Regulator | Check karne wala | Data protection authority |
| Unregulated | Bina rule ke | Aisa code ya market jahan koi check na ho |

**Memory Trick:** Think of a **"Regulator"** as a "Traffic Police" for data. Just like traffic police keeps the road safe, a regulator keeps the digital world safe!
