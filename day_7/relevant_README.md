Hello! I am your English language instructor. Today, we will learn about the word family **"Relevant."** This is a very important word in academic writing and Computer Science. Let's start!

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:** None (This word does not have a verb form).
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Relevance
    *   Irrelevance
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Relevant
    *   Irrelevant
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   Relevantly (less common)
    *   Irrelevantly (less common)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Relevance (Noun):** The quality of being connected to the topic.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ka topic se talluq ya connection hona.
*   **Irrelevance (Noun):** The quality of NOT being connected to the topic.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ka topic se koi talluq na hona.
*   **Relevant (Adjective):** Closely connected to the matter at hand.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo baat ho rahi ho, us se milta julta ya zaroori.
*   **Irrelevant (Adjective):** Not connected to the matter at hand.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo baat se bahar ho ya be-tukki ho.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Relevant:**
    1.  **Applicable:** Jo apply ho sake (laagu).
    2.  **Pertinent:** Jo baat se mutaliq ho.
*   **Irrelevant:**
    1.  **Unrelated:** Jis ka koi talluq na ho.
    2.  **Extraneous:** Jo zaroori na ho ya bahar ki cheez ho.

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Relevance:**
    1. The relevance of this data is very high for our research. (Hamari research ke liye is data ki ahmiyat/talluq bohat zyada hai.)
    2. We must check the relevance of these files. (Hamein in files ka topic se talluq check karna chahiye.)
*   **Irrelevance:**
    1. The professor ignored the student's comment due to its irrelevance. (Professor ne student ki baat ko nazar-andaz kar diya kyunke wo topic se hat kar thi.)
    2. The irrelevance of the old code caused confusion. (Purane code ka topic se koi talluq na hone ki wajah se confusion hui.)
*   **Relevant:**
    1. Please provide relevant information for the project. (Baraye meharbani project ke liye zaroori/mutaliqah maloomat dein.)
    2. The search engine shows relevant results. (Search engine topic se milti julti results dikhata hai.)
*   **Irrelevant:**
    1. This paragraph is irrelevant to the research paper. (Ye paragraph research paper ke topic se bahar hai.)
    2. Do not include irrelevant details in the code comments. (Code comments mein faltu ya be-tukki tafseelat mat likhein.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. This algorithm is **relevant** to our study. (Ye algorithm hamari study se mutaliq hai.)
2. The **relevance** of AI in modern medicine is clear. (Jadeed tib mein AI ki ahmiyat wazeh hai.)
3. Avoid adding **irrelevant** data to the database. (Database mein faltu data shamil karne se bachein.)
4. The **irrelevance** of his answer surprised the teacher. (Uske jawab ka topic se talluq na hone par teacher hairan hue.)
5. Is this source **relevant** for your final thesis? (Kya ye source aapke final thesis ke liye zaroori hai?)
6. The scientist removed **irrelevant** variables from the experiment. (Scientist ne experiment se ghair zaroori variables hata diye.)
7. We need to discuss the **relevance** of this new software. (Hamein is naye software ki ahmiyat par baat karni hogi.)
8. His argument was **irrelevant** to the main topic. (Us ki daleel main topic se hat kar thi.)
9. Keep your report focused on **relevant** facts only. (Apni report ko sirf zaroori haqayiq tak mehdood rakhein.)
10. The **irrelevance** of the input caused the system to crash. (Input ka topic se talluq na hone ki wajah se system crash ho gaya.)
11. Are these search results **relevant** to your query? (Kya ye search results aap ki query se mutaliq hain?)
12. The team discussed the **relevance** of the new security patch. (Team ne naye security patch ki ahmiyat par baat ki.)
13. That information is **irrelevant** to this specific project. (Wo maloomat is khaas project ke liye ghair zaroori hain.)
14. The **relevance** of cybersecurity is growing daily. (Cybersecurity ki ahmiyat roz ba roz barh rahi hai.)
15. Please filter out all **irrelevant** search results. (Baraye meharbani tamam ghair zaroori search results ko hata dein.)

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

| Word | Simple Meaning | Academic/CS Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Relevant** | Connected to the topic | Used for data, facts, or code. |
| **Irrelevant** | Not connected | Used for extra, useless data. |
| **Relevance** | The connection | Used to check importance. |
| **Irrelevance** | No connection | Used to describe bad input. |

**Memory Trick:**
*   **"Re-"** means again or back. Think of **"Relevant"** as "Re-lever" (bringing the point back to the lever/main topic).
*   **"Ir-"** at the start usually means "NOT." So, **Irrelevant** = NOT relevant.
