### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Site (to site)
    *   Resite (less common)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Site
    *   Sites (plural)
    *   Siting
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Situated
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   (None)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Site (Verb):** To place something in a specific location.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ko kisi khas jagah par rakhna ya banana.
*   **Site (Noun):** A place where something happens or exists (like a website or a building place).
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh jagah jahan koi kaam ho raha ho, ya internet par koi page (website).
*   **Siting (Noun):** The act of choosing a place for something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jagah chunne ka amal.
*   **Situated (Adjective):** Where something is located.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Yeh batana ke koi cheez kahan waqay hai.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Site (Noun):**
    *   **Location:** Jagah/maqam.
    *   **Position:** Position ya jagah.
*   **Site (Verb):**
    *   **Locate:** Kisi cheez ki jagah tay karna.
    *   **Place:** Rakhna ya qaim karna.

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Site (Verb):**
    *   The engineers decided to site the new server room in the basement.
    *   *Engineers ne faisla kiya ke naya server room basement mein banaya jaye.*
    *   We must site the antenna where it gets the best signal.
    *   *Hamein antenna wahan lagana chahiye jahan signal behtareen ho.*
*   **Site (Noun):**
    *   This website provides free tutorials for coding.
    *   *Yeh website coding ke liye muft tutorials deti hai.*
    *   The construction site for the new data center is ready.
    *   *Naye data center ki tameer ki jagah tayyar hai.*
*   **Siting (Noun):**
    *   The siting of the computer lab is important for student access.
    *   *Computer lab ki jagah chunna students ki sahulat ke liye ahem hai.*
    *   Proper siting of hardware prevents overheating.
    *   *Hardware ko sahi jagah rakhne se woh garam nahi hota.*
*   **Situated (Adjective):**
    *   The main server is situated in the secure room.
    *   *Main server mehfooz kamray mein waqay hai.*
    *   Our research lab is situated near the university library.
    *   *Hamara research lab university library ke qareeb waqay hai.*

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The website is very fast. (Yeh website bohot tez hai.)
2. Please site the database on a secure server. (Baraye meharbani database ko aik mehfooz server par rakhein.)
3. Where is the new office situated? (Naya office kahan waqay hai?)
4. The siting of these sensors is critical for data accuracy. (In sensors ki jagah chunna data ki durusti ke liye ahem hai.)
5. Many educational sites offer free courses. (Bohat si taleemi sites muft courses deti hain.)
6. We situated the router in the center of the room. (Hum ne router ko kamray ke beech mein rakha.)
7. The project site was moved last week. (Project ki jagah pichle hafte tabdeel kar di gayi.)
8. Good siting helps in better networking. (Sahi jagah chunne se networking behtar hoti hai.)
9. The university has many sites for research. (University ke paas research ke liye bohat si jagah hain.)
10. Is the server situated in a cool area? (Kya server thandi jagah par waqay hai?)
11. The developer updated the site content. (Developer ne site ka content update kiya.)
12. Proper siting of cables reduces interference. (Cables ko sahi jagah lagane se kharabi kam hoti hai.)
13. Several sites were tested for the new network. (Naye network ke liye kayi jagah check ki gayi.)
14. The lab is situated on the second floor. (Lab doosri manzil par waqay hai.)
15. He is working on a site optimization project. (Woh site ko behtar banane ke project par kaam kar raha hai.)
