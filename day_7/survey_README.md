### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Survey (Base form)
    *   Surveyed (Past tense)
    *   Surveying (Continuous form)
    *   Surveys (Third-person singular)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Survey (The act of gathering information)
    *   Surveyor (The person who does the survey)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Surveyed (Used as an adjective, e.g., "the surveyed data")
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   *None commonly used in academic English.*

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Survey (Verb):** To look at or study something carefully to get information.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ka jaiza lena ya maloomat jama karna.
*   **Survey (Noun):** A list of questions given to people to get their opinions.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Sawalat ki fehrist jo logon se raaye janne ke liye puchi jati hai.
*   **Surveyor (Noun):** A person who does the survey.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh shakhs jo survey karta hai.
*   **Surveyed (Verb/Adj):** Something that has been studied or checked.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh cheez jis ka jaiza liya ja chuka ho.
*   **Surveying (Verb):** The process of doing a survey.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Survey karne ka amal.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Examine (Verb):** To look at closely. (*Roman Urdu: Ghaur se dekhna ya jaiza lena.*)
*   **Assess (Verb):** To judge or evaluate. (*Roman Urdu: Qadar ya ehmiyat ka andaza lagana.*)
*   **Investigate (Verb):** To study a subject in detail. (*Roman Urdu: Tehqeeq karna.*)

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Survey (Verb):** Researchers survey students to understand their coding habits.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Researchers talaba ka jaiza lete hain taake unki coding ki aadaton ko samajh saken.
*   **Survey (Noun):** The survey shows that Python is the most popular language.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Survey batata hai ke Python sab se maqbool zabaan (language) hai.
*   **Surveyed (Verb):** We surveyed 500 websites for this security report.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Hum ne is security report ke liye 500 websites ka jaiza liya.
*   **Surveying (Verb):** He is currently surveying the performance of new cloud servers.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh filhal naye cloud servers ki karkardagi (performance) ka jaiza le raha hai.
*   **Surveys (Noun/Verb):** Frequent surveys help improve software quality.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Baar baar kiye gaye surveys software ki quality ko behtar banane mein madad karte hain.
*   **Surveyor (Noun):** The surveyor analyzed the network traffic data.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Surveyor ne network traffic ke data ka tajziya kiya.

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. We will **survey** the users about the new app. (Hum naye app ke bare mein users ka jaiza lenge.)
2. The team **surveyed** the entire system architecture. (Team ne poore system architecture ka jaiza liya.)
3. **Surveying** large datasets takes a lot of time. (Baray datasets ka jaiza lene mein bahut waqt lagta hai.)
4. The **survey** results were very surprising. (Survey ke nataij (results) bahut hairan kun thay.)
5. Many **surveys** suggest that AI is growing fast. (Bahut se surveys batate hain ke AI tezi se barh raha hai.)
6. He is a professional **surveyor** of database systems. (Woh database systems ka mahir surveyor hai.)
7. We **surveyed** the students before the exam. (Hum ne imtehan se pehle talaba ka jaiza liya.)
8. The **surveyed** group preferred using Linux. (Jaiza liye gaye group ne Linux istemal karna pasand kiya.)
9. Start **surveying** the error logs now. (Abhi error logs ka jaiza lena shuru karein.)
10. This **survey** covers modern web security. (Yeh survey jadeed web security ka ahata karta hai.)
11. Are you **surveying** the market for new laptops? (Kya aap naye laptops ke liye market ka jaiza le rahe hain?)
12. The **surveyed** data is very accurate. (Jaiza liya gaya data bahut durust hai.)
13. Our **surveys** indicate a need for faster internet. (Hamare surveys tezi internet ki zaroorat ki taraf ishara karte hain.)
14. The **surveyor** finished the report yesterday. (Surveyor ne kal report mukammal kar li.)
15. Always **survey** the problem before writing code. (Code likhne se pehle hamesha maslay ka jaiza lein.)
