Hello! I am your English language instructor. Today, we will learn about the word **"Purchase"**. This is a very important word in academic and computer science contexts. Let's start!

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Purchase (base form)
    *   Purchased (past tense)
    *   Purchasing (present participle)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Purchase (the act of buying)
    *   Purchaser (the person who buys)
    *   Purchasers (plural: more than one person who buys)
    *   Purchases (plural: more than one thing bought)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Purchase (Verb):** To buy something using money.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ko paise de kar khareedna.
*   **Purchased (Verb):** Bought something in the past.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Pehle khareed chuke hain.
*   **Purchasing (Verb):** The action of buying something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Khareedne ka amal.
*   **Purchase (Noun):** An item that you have bought.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh cheez jo aap ne khareedi hai.
*   **Purchaser (Noun):** A person who buys something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Khareedne wala shakhs.
*   **Purchasers (Noun):** Many people who buy things.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada khareedne wale log.
*   **Purchases (Noun):** Many items that were bought.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Bahut saari khareedi hui cheezein.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

1.  **Buy:** Simple word for purchase. (*Roman Urdu: Khareedna*)
2.  **Acquire:** To get or obtain something. (*Roman Urdu: Hasil karna*)
3.  **Procure:** To get something, usually for a business. (*Roman Urdu: Intezaam kar ke hasil karna*)

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Purchase (Verb):** Universities purchase software for students.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Universities students ke liye software khareedti hain.
*   **Purchase (Noun):** The purchase of a new server is necessary.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Naya server khareedna zaroori hai.
*   **Purchased (Verb):** The lab purchased new computers last week.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Lab ne pichle hafte naye computers khareede.
*   **Purchasing (Verb):** We are purchasing cloud storage for the project.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Hum project ke liye cloud storage khareed rahe hain.
*   **Purchaser (Noun):** The purchaser must sign the contract.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Khareedne wale ko contract sign karna hoga.
*   **Purchasers (Noun):** Many purchasers prefer fast delivery.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Zyada tar khareedne wale fast delivery pasand karte hain.
*   **Purchases (Noun):** The company tracks all online purchases.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Company tamam online khareedari ko track karti hai.

---

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The department will **purchase** a new high-speed router. (*Department naya router khareede ga.*)
2. We **purchased** the license for the software yesterday. (*Hum ne kal software ka license khareeda.*)
3. **Purchasing** expensive hardware requires approval. (*Mehnga hardware khareedne ke liye ijazat chahiye.*)
4. The **purchaser** is responsible for the shipping costs. (*Khareedne wala shipping ke kharche ka zimmedar hai.*)
5. Online **purchases** have increased this year. (*Is saal online khareedari barh gayi hai.*)
6. Many **purchasers** look for good reviews first. (*Bahut se khareedne wale pehle achay reviews dekhte hain.*)
7. You must **purchase** the subscription to access the data. (*Data access karne ke liye aap ko subscription khareedni hogi.*)
8. The **purchasing** process is very simple on our website. (*Hamari website par khareedne ka amal bahut aasaan hai.*)
9. He **purchased** a new domain name for his blog. (*Usne apne blog ke liye naya domain name khareeda.*)
10. All **purchasers** will receive an email receipt. (*Tamam khareedne walon ko email receipt milegi.*)
11. The university **purchased** fifty new laptops. (*University ne pachaas naye laptops khareede.*)
12. **Purchasing** cloud services saves money for the lab. (*Cloud services khareedne se lab ke paise bachtay hain.*)
13. The **purchaser** needs to provide their ID. (*Khareedne wale ko apni ID deni hogi.*)
14. These **purchases** are for research purposes only. (*Yeh khareedari sirf research ke maqsad ke liye hai.*)
15. We are **purchasing** advanced security tools. (*Hum advanced security tools khareed rahe hain.*)
