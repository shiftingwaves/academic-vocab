### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Structure (Verb/Noun):** To build or arrange something; the way things are organized.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ko tarteeb dena ya uska dhancha/banawat.
*   **Restructure (Verb):** To organize something again in a new way.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ko dobara naye tareeqe se tarteeb dena.
*   **Structural (Adj):** Related to the physical or logical shape of something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Banawat ya dhanchay se mutaliq.
*   **Structured (Adj):** Organized or planned carefully.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo cheez nizam ke tehat ya tarteeb se ho.
*   **Unstructured (Adj):** Not organized; messy or random.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo cheez be-tarteeb ho, jis ka koi khaas dhancha na ho.
*   **Structurally (Adv):** In a way that relates to the structure.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Banawati tor par.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

1.  **Structure (Noun):**
    *   **Framework:** Dhancha ya bunyadi nizam.
    *   **Organization:** Intezaam ya tarteeb.
2.  **Restructure (Verb):**
    *   **Reorganize:** Dobara tarteeb dena.
    *   **Revamp:** Naye siray se banana.

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Structure:**
    *   The structure of this program is very simple. (Is program ki banawat bohat aasaan hai.)
    *   We need a clear structure for our research paper. (Hamein apne research paper ke liye aik saaf dhancha chahiye.)
*   **Restructure:**
    *   The team decided to restructure the database. (Team ne database ko dobara tarteeb dene ka faisla kiya.)
    *   They restructured the code to make it faster. (Unhon ne code ko tez karne ke liye dobara arrange kiya.)
*   **Structural:**
    *   There is a structural error in the algorithm. (Algorithm mein banawati ghalti hai.)
    *   Structural changes are needed for this software. (Is software ke liye banawati tabdeeliyaan zaroori hain.)
*   **Structured:**
    *   This is a structured approach to coding. (Yeh coding karne ka aik nizam-mand tareeqa hai.)
    *   Structured data is easier to analyze. (Tarteeb-shuda data ko analyze karna aasaan hota hai.)
*   **Unstructured:**
    *   The internet contains a lot of unstructured data. (Internet par bohat sa be-tarteeb data maujood hai.)
    *   Unstructured text is hard for computers to read. (Be-tarteeb text ko computers ke liye parhna mushkil hota hai.)
*   **Structurally:**
    *   The two systems are structurally different. (Dono systems banawati tor par mukhtalif hain.)
    *   The file is structurally sound. (Yeh file banawati tor par bilkul theek hai.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The software **structure** needs improvement. (Software ki banawat ko behtar banane ki zaroorat hai.)
2. We are **structuring** the new project modules. (Hum naye project modules ko tarteeb de rahe hain.)
3. The company **restructures** its servers every year. (Company har saal apne servers ko dobara tarteeb deti hai.)
4. **Unstructured** data comes from social media. (Be-tarteeb data social media se aata hai.)
5. The **structural** design of the app is great. (App ka banawati design bohat acha hai.)
6. We have **structured** the lesson into three parts. (Hum ne sabaq ko teen hisson mein tarteeb diya hai.)
7. The team is **restructuring** the entire network. (Team poore network ko dobara tarteeb de rahi hai.)
8. **Structurally**, this code is very strong. (Banawati tor par, yeh code bohat mazboot hai.)
9. Many **structures** in programming help save time. (Programming mein bohat se dhanchay waqt bachane mein madad karte hain.)
10. The professor **restructured** the research methodology. (Professor ne research ke tareeqe-kaar ko dobara tarteeb diya.)
11. **Structured** queries are used in SQL. (SQL mein tarteeb-shuda queries istemaal hoti hain.)
12. Data can be **structured** or **unstructured**. (Data tarteeb-shuda ya be-tarteeb ho sakta hai.)
13. The **structural** integrity of the system is vital. (System ki banawati mazbooti zaroori hai.)
14. They **restructured** the company website last month. (Unhon ne pichle mahine company ki website ko dobara tarteeb diya.)
15. A good **structure** leads to better results. (Aik achi tarteeb behtar nateeja deti hai.)
