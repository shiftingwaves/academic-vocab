### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:** source, sources (present), sourced (past/past participle), sourcing (continuous)
*   **Noun forms:** source, sources (plural)
*   **Adjective forms:** sourced (e.g., "well-sourced")
*   **Adverb forms:** (None common)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Source (Noun):** The place where something comes from.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh jagah jahan se koi cheez shuru hoti hai ya milti hai.
*   **Source (Verb):** To find where something comes from or to get something from a place.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ka asal pata lagana ya kisi jagah se cheez hasil karna.
*   **Sources (Noun - Plural):** More than one place where information or things come from.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada jagah jahan se maloomat milti hain.
*   **Sourced (Verb - Past):** Already found or obtained from a specific place.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Pehle se hasil ki gayi cheez.
*   **Sourcing (Verb - Continuous):** The process of finding or getting materials/info.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Maloomat ya cheezon ko dhoondne ka amal.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

1.  **Origin:** (Asal) – Where it starts.
2.  **Resource:** (Zariya) – A supply of something useful.
3.  **Reference:** (Hawala) – A source used for information.

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Source (Noun):** The internet is a primary source of data for this research.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Internet is research ke liye data ka bunyadi zariya hai.
*   **Source (Verb):** Developers must source high-quality code libraries for their projects.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Developers ko apne projects ke liye achi quality ki code libraries dhoondni chahiye.
*   **Sources (Noun):** You must cite all your sources in your essay.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Aapko apne essay mein tamam sources ka hawala dena chahiye.
*   **Sourced (Verb):** The images for this website were sourced from a free database.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Is website ki images ek free database se li gayi hain.
*   **Sourcing (Verb):** Sourcing reliable information is important for a good paper.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Achi research paper ke liye qabil-e-aitamad maloomat dhoondna zaroori hai.

---

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1.  Always check the **source** of your data. (Apne data ka zariya hamesha check karein.)
2.  We **source** our hardware from trusted vendors. (Hum apna hardware bharosemand vendors se lete hain.)
3.  The **sources** used in this paper are very old. (Is paper mein istemal hone wale sources bahut purane hain.)
4.  He **sourced** the missing parts from an online store. (Usne missing parts ek online store se hasil kiye.)
5.  **Sourcing** accurate information takes time. (Sahi maloomat dhoondne mein waqt lagta hai.)
6.  The **source** code is available on GitHub. (Source code GitHub par majood hai.)
7.  Many **sources** agree on this technical theory. (Bahut sare sources is technical theory par ittefaq karte hain.)
8.  The company is **sourcing** new talent for the AI project. (Company AI project ke liye naye talent ko dhoond rahi hai.)
9.  This report is well-**sourced**. (Yeh report ache sources se li gayi hai.)
10. Can you identify the **source** of the error? (Kya aap error ki wajah/jagah bata sakte hain?)
11. We are **sourcing** components for the robot. (Hum robot ke liye parts dhoond rahe hain.)
12. The student failed to list his **sources**. (Student apne sources likhna bhool gaya.)
13. Every **source** must be verified. (Har source ko check karna zaroori hai.)
14. They **sourced** the research from a library. (Unhone research library se hasil ki.)
15. **Sourcing** global data is challenging. (Global data dhoondna mushkil kaam hai.)
