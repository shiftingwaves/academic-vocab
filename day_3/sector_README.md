### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Sectorize (less common)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Sector
    *   Sectors (plural)
    *   Sectorization (less common)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Sectoral
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   Sectorally (less common)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Sector (Noun):** A specific part or area of a larger system or economy.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi bari cheez ya system ka ek khaas hissa ya area.
*   **Sectors (Plural Noun):** More than one part or area.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada hissay ya areas.
*   **Sectoral (Adjective):** Relating to a specific sector.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi khaas sector se taluq rakhne wala.
*   **Sectorize (Verb):** To divide something into sectors.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ko hisson mein baantna.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Segment:** (Hissa/Tukra)
*   **Area:** (Ilaka/Shoba)

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Sector:**
    1. The software sector is growing very fast. (Software ka shoba bohat tezi se barh raha hai.)
    2. A hard drive is divided into many sectors. (Hard drive bohat saare sectors mein taqseem hoti hai.)
*   **Sectors:**
    1. We need to analyze different sectors of the economy. (Humein economy ke mukhtalif sectors ka jaiza lena hoga.)
    2. Modern computers organize data into small sectors. (Jadeed computers data ko chotay sectors mein organize karte hain.)
*   **Sectoral:**
    1. The government released a sectoral report on IT. (Hukumat ne IT par ek sectoral report jari ki.)
    2. We see sectoral changes in the tech industry. (Hum tech industry mein sectoral tabdeeliyan dekh rahe hain.)
*   **Sectorize:**
    1. You should sectorize the disk to improve performance. (Performance behtar karne ke liye aapko disk ko sectorize karna chahiye.)
    2. It is difficult to sectorize this large database. (Is baray database ko sectorize karna mushkil hai.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

11. **Sectoral** shifts often happen in technology. (Technology mein aksar sectoral tabdeeliyan aati hain.)
