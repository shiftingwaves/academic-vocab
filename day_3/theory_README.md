### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Theory (Noun):** An idea or a set of rules to explain how something works.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ke kaam karne ka usool ya idea jo sabit karne ki koshish ki jaye.
*   **Theories (Noun - Plural):** More than one theory.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada theories ya ideas.
*   **Theorist (Noun):** A person who creates or studies theories.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh shakhs jo naye ideas ya theories banata hai.
*   **Theorists (Noun - Plural):** More than one theorist.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada log jo theories par kaam karte hain.
*   **Theorize (Verb):** To think about or create a theory.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ke bare mein sochna ya theory banana.
*   **Theoretical (Adjective):** Based on ideas, not on practical experience.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Sirf soch ya papers tak mehdood, practical nahi.
*   **Theoretically (Adverb):** In a way that is based on theory.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Nazriyati taur par (soch ke mutabiq).

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Theory:**
    *   Hypothesis (Ek aisa idea jo abhi proof hona baqi hai)
    *   Concept (Ek soch ya idea)
*   **Theoretical:**
    *   Abstract (Jo sirf dimagh mein ho, physical na ho)
    *   Speculative (Jo sirf andazon par mabni ho)

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Theory:**
    1. The theory of computation is important for CS students. (Computation ki theory CS students ke liye zaroori hai.)
    2. This new theory explains how AI models learn. (Yeh nayi theory samjhati hai ke AI models kaise seekhte hain.)
*   **Theories:**
    1. Scientists have many theories about quantum computing. (Scientists ke paas quantum computing ke bare mein bahut si theories hain.)
    2. We compared different theories in our research paper. (Humne apne research paper mein mukhtalif theories ka mawazna kiya.)
*   **Theorist:**
    1. The famous theorist published a new paper today. (Mashhoor theorist ne aaj ek naya paper publish kiya.)
    2. Every theorist needs to test their ideas. (Har theorist ko apne ideas ko test karna chahiye.)
*   **Theorists:**
    1. Computer theorists often use mathematics. (Computer theorists aksar math ka istemal karte hain.)
    2. Many theorists disagree on this topic. (Bahut se theorists is topic par ikhtilaf rakhte hain.)
*   **Theorize:**
    1. Researchers theorize that robots will become smarter. (Researchers yeh theory banate hain ke robots zyada smart ho jayenge.)
    2. We cannot theorize without data. (Hum data ke baghair theory nahi bana sakte.)
*   **Theoretical:**
    1. The project is still in the theoretical stage. (Yeh project abhi sirf theoretical stage par hai.)
    2. This is a theoretical model for network security. (Yeh network security ke liye ek theoretical model hai.)
*   **Theoretically:**
    1. It is theoretically possible to build this system. (Is system ko banana theoretically mumkin hai.)
    2. The code should work theoretically. (Code ko theoretically kaam karna chahiye.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The **theory** behind this algorithm is very complex. (Is algorithm ke peeche ki theory bahut mushkil hai.)
2. Many **theorists** believe that AI will change the world. (Bahut se theorists ka manna hai ke AI duniya badal dega.)
3. We need to **theorize** about future network problems. (Hamein future ke network masail par theory banani chahiye.)
4. The **theoretical** framework of this paper is strong. (Is paper ka theoretical framework mazboot hai.)
5. **Theoretically**, your program should run faster. (Theoretically, aapka program tez chalna chahiye.)
6. She is a top **theorist** in the field of cryptography. (Woh cryptography ke field mein top theorist hai.)
7. These **theories** were tested in a lab. (In theories ko lab mein test kiya gaya.)
8. Does your research **theorize** a new way to store data? (Kya aapki research data store karne ka naya tareeqa theorize karti hai?)
9. The **theoretical** limit of this processor is high. (Is processor ki theoretical limit zyada hai.)
10. Can you **theorize** why the server crashed? (Kya aap theory bana sakte hain ke server kyun crash hua?)
11. The **theorists** presented their findings at the conference. (Theorists ne apni findings conference mein pesh ki.)
12. This is just a **theory**; we need proof. (Yeh sirf ek theory hai; hamein saboot chahiye.)
13. The **theoretical** approach helps us understand bugs. (Theoretical approach hamein bugs samajhne mein madad karti hai.)
14. They are **theorizing** about the next version of the software. (Woh software ke agle version ke bare mein theorize kar rahe hain.)
15. **Theoretically**, this is the best solution. (Theoretically, yeh behtareen hal hai.)
