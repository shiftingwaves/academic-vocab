### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Estimate (Verb):** To calculate or guess a value. (Kisi cheez ka andaza lagana).
*   **Estimating (Verb):** The act of guessing a value. (Andaza laga rahe hain).
*   **Overestimate (Verb):** To guess too high. (Zaroorat se zyada andaza lagana).
*   **Overestimated (Verb):** Guessed too high in the past. (Pehle zyada andaza lagaya).
*   **Overestimating (Verb):** The act of guessing too high. (Zyada andaza laga rahe hain).
*   **Underestimate (Verb):** To guess too low. (Zaroorat se kam andaza lagana).
*   **Underestimated (Verb):** Guessed too low in the past. (Pehle kam andaza lagaya).
*   **Underestimating (Verb):** The act of guessing too low. (Kam andaza laga rahe hain).
*   **Estimation (Noun):** The result of guessing. (Andaza ya calculation).
*   **Estimations (Noun):** Multiple guesses or calculations. (Ek se zyada andazay).
*   **Estimated (Adjective):** Something that has been calculated. (Jo andaza lagaya gaya ho).

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

1.  **Calculate:** To find a number using math. (Hisab lagana).
2.  **Assess:** To judge the value or quality of something. (Jaiza lena).
3.  **Gauge:** To measure or estimate. (Napna ya andaza karna).

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Estimate:** We need to **estimate** the time for the code to run. (Hamein code run hone ke waqt ka andaza lagana hai.)
*   **Estimated:** The **estimated** cost of the project is low. (Project ki andazan cost kam hai.)
*   **Overestimate:** Do not **overestimate** the speed of the server. (Server ki speed ka had se zyada andaza mat lagayen.)
*   **Underestimate:** Never **underestimate** the complexity of AI models. (AI models ki mushkil ka kabhi kam andaza mat lagayen.)
*   **Estimation:** My **estimation** shows that the data is accurate. (Mera andaza batata hai ke data sahi hai.)
