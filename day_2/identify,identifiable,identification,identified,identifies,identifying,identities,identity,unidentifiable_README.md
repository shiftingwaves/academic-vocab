---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:** identify, identifies, identified, identifying
*   **Noun forms:** identity, identities, identification
*   **Adjective forms:** identifiable, unidentifiable
*   **Adverb forms:** (None common)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Identify (Verb):** To find or show who or what someone or something is. (Kisi cheez ya shakhs ko pehchan-na ya dhoond lena.)
*   **Identifies (Verb):** Third-person singular form of identify. (Yeh wohi kaam hai, lekin singular subject ke liye.)
*   **Identified (Verb):** Past tense of identify. (Jo pehchan liya gaya ho.)
*   **Identifying (Verb):** Showing or finding something (continuous). (Pehchan karne ka amal.)
*   **Identity (Noun):** Who a person is or what a thing is. (Kisi ki pehchan/shanakht.)
*   **Identities (Noun):** Plural of identity. (Ek se zyada pehchanen.)
*   **Identification (Noun):** The process of showing who/what someone is. (Shanakht ka amal/ID card.)
*   **Identifiable (Adjective):** Able to be recognized or found. (Jo pehchana ja sake.)
*   **Unidentifiable (Adjective):** Not able to be recognized or found. (Jo pehchana na ja sake.)

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Recognize:** Pehchan-na.
*   **Detect:** Pata lagana ya dhoondna.
*   **Determine:** Tay karna ya maloom karna.

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

1.  **Identify:** The software helps to **identify** bugs in the code. (Yeh software code mein bugs ko pehchanne mein madad karta hai.)
2.  **Identifies:** The system **identifies** the user by their password. (System password ke zariye user ki shanakht karta hai.)
3.  **Identified:** We **identified** a problem in the network. (Humne network mein ek masla dhoond liya.)
4.  **Identifying:** **Identifying** security threats is important for a developer. (Security threats ko pehchanna developer ke liye zaroori hai.)
5.  **Identity:** The **identity** of the hacker remains unknown. (Hacker ki pehchan abhi tak maloom nahi.)
6.  **Identities:** The database stores the digital **identities** of all students. (Database sab students ki digital identities store karta hai.)
7.  **Identification:** Proper **identification** is required to access the server. (Server access karne ke liye sahi shanakht zaroori hai.)
8.  **Identifiable:** The error in the script is clearly **identifiable**. (Script mein ghalti saaf pehchani ja sakti hai.)
9.  **Unidentifiable:** The damaged file is currently **unidentifiable**. (Kharab file filhal pehchan ke qabil nahi hai.)
