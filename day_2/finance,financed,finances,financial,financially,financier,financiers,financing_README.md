---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Finance (Verb):** To provide money for a project. (Kisi project ke liye paise dena.)
*   **Finance (Noun):** The management of money. (Paise ka intizam.)
*   **Financed (Verb):** Provided money in the past. (Pichle waqt mein paise diye.)
*   **Finances (Noun/Verb):** The money a person or company has. (Kisi ke paas maujood raqam.)
*   **Financing (Verb/Noun):** The act of providing money. (Paise dene ka amal.)
*   **Financier (Noun):** A person who gives large amounts of money for business. (Woh shakhs jo bara sarmaya deta hai.)
*   **Financial (Adjective):** Related to money. (Paise se mutaliq.)
*   **Financially (Adverb):** In a way that relates to money. (Paise ke lehaz se.)

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Finance (Verb):**
    *   Fund (Paise dena)
    *   Back (Support karna)
*   **Financial (Adjective):**
    *   Economic (Maashi)
    *   Monetary (Raqam se mutaliq)

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Finance (Verb):** The university will finance the new AI lab. (University naye AI lab ke liye paise degi.)
*   **Finance (Noun):** He is studying finance at the university. (Woh university mein finance parh raha hai.)
*   **Financed:** The research project was financed by a tech company. (Research project ko ek tech company ne finance kiya.)
*   **Finances:** We need to check our project finances carefully. (Humein apne project ke paise (finances) ghor se dekhne honge.)
*   **Financing:** Financing for this software startup is difficult. (Is software startup ke liye paise ka intizam karna mushkil hai.)
*   **Financier:** The financier agreed to support the new app. (Financier naye app ko support karne ke liye maan gaya.)
*   **Financial:** The project has many financial problems. (Project ko bohat se maali (financial) masail hain.)
*   **Financially:** The lab is now financially independent. (Lab ab paise ke lehaz se azad hai.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The government will **finance** the new computer lab. (Hukumat naye computer lab ke liye paise degi.)
2. We need better **financial** planning for our CS research. (Humein apni CS research ke liye behtar maali planning ki zaroorat hai.)
3. The company is **financially** stable after the new update. (Nayi update ke baad company paise ke lehaz se mazboot hai.)
4. Who is **financing** this big data project? (Is big data project ke liye paise kaun de raha hai?)
5. The **financier** invested in the AI startup. (Financier ne AI startup mein sarmaya lagaya.)
6. Our department’s **finances** are managed by the head office. (Hamare department ke paise head office sambhalta hai.)
7. Many **financiers** are interested in blockchain technology. (Bohat se financiers blockchain technology mein dilchaspi rakhte hain.)
8. The startup was **financed** by a venture capital firm. (Startup ko ek venture capital firm ne paise diye.)
9. **Financial** reports show that the IT sector is growing. (Maali reports dikhati hain ke IT sector barh raha hai.)
10. We are **financially** supporting the student research team. (Hum student research team ki paise ke zariye madad kar rahe hain.)
11. **Financing** a large project requires a good plan. (Bare project ke liye paise ka intizam karne ke liye achi planning chahiye.)
12. The **finance** department approved the budget for the server. (Finance department ne server ke liye budget manzoor kar liya.)
13. Is the project **financially** viable? (Kya project paise ke lehaz se faidamand hai?)
14. The **financiers** want to see the results first. (Financiers pehle natijay dekhna chahte hain.)
15. Proper **financing** is key to success in tech. (Tech mein kamyabi ke liye sahi tareeqe se paise ka intizam zaroori hai.)
