### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   None (In academic English, "income" is almost always used as a noun).
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Income (base word)
    *   Incomes (plural)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Low-income (used to describe people or areas)
    *   High-income (used to describe people or areas)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   None.

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Income:** Money that a person or a company earns from work or business.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh paisa jo aap kaam ya business se kamate hain.
*   **Incomes:** More than one source or amount of money earned.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jab ek se zyada logon ki ya mukhtalif sources ki kamai ki baat ho.
*   **Low-income:** Earning a small amount of money.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kam aamdani wala (aisa shakhs ya area jahan paisay kam hon).
*   **High-income:** Earning a large amount of money.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Zyada aamdani wala (amir ya achi kamai wala).

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Earnings:**
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo paisa aap ne mehnat se kamaya ho.
*   **Revenue:** (Used for companies/businesses)
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Company ki kul aamdani ya sale se ane wala paisa.
*   **Salary:**
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Tankhwah (jo har mahine milti hai).

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Income:**
    1. The study shows how income affects student performance. (Study dikhati hai ke income student ki performance par kaise asar karti hai.)
    2. We must record the annual income of this startup. (Hamein is startup ki saalana aamdani record karni chahiye.)
*   **Incomes:**
    1. The research compares the incomes of software engineers in different countries. (Research mukhtalif mumalik mein software engineers ki incomes ka mawazna karti hai.)
    2. Many families have multiple incomes in this city. (Is shehar mein bohat se khandanon ki aamdani ke zariye ek se zyada hain.)
*   **Low-income:**
    1. The app provides free education to low-income students. (Ye app kam aamdani wale students ko muft taleem deti hai.)
    2. Researchers are studying low-income areas to improve internet access. (Researchers internet ki sahulat behtar banane ke liye kam aamdani wale ilaqon ka jaiza le rahe hain.)
*   **High-income:**
    1. High-income countries have better access to advanced technology. (Zyada aamdani wale mumalik mein advanced technology tak rasai behtar hai.)
    2. The software is popular among high-income professionals. (Ye software zyada aamdani wale professionals mein maqbool hai.)
