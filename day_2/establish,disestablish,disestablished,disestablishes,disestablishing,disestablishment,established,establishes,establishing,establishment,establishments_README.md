### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Establish:** To start or create something (like a system or rule).
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ko shuru karna ya bunyaad rakhna.
*   **Establishes:** Same as 'establish', used for singular subjects (he/she/it).
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ye bhi 'establish' hi hai, jo singular subject ke liye use hota hai.
*   **Establishing:** Doing the act of starting something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ko shuru kar rahe hona.
*   **Established:** Something that is already started or proven.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo pehle se shuru ho chuka ho ya sabit ho chuka ho.
*   **Disestablish:** To end the status of something; to stop it.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ki bunyaad ko khatam kar dena.
*   **Disestablishes/Disestablishing/Disestablished:** Different tenses of 'disestablish'.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ye 'disestablish' ki mukhtalif forms hain.
*   **Establishment:** A place, organization, or the act of creating.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Koi idara ya kisi cheez ko qaim karne ka amal.
*   **Establishments:** Plural of establishment.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada idaray ya organizations.

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Establish:**
    1. **Set up:** Bunyaad rakhna.
    2. **Found:** Kisi cheez ki ibtida karna.
    3. **Initiate:** Shuruat karna.

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Establish:** We must establish a secure connection. (Hamein ek mehfooz connection banana hoga.)
*   **Establish:** The study aims to establish a new theory. (Study ka maqsad ek naya theory qaim karna hai.)
*   **Establishes:** The firewall establishes a barrier. (Firewall ek rukawat qaim karti hai.)
*   **Establishes:** This code establishes the user profile. (Ye code user profile banata hai.)
*   **Establishing:** We are establishing a new server. (Hum ek naya server shuru kar rahe hain.)
*   **Establishing:** Establishing rules is important for data safety. (Rules banana data safety ke liye zaroori hai.)
*   **Established:** The established protocol is very fast. (Jo protocol pehle se bana hua hai wo bohot tez hai.)
*   **Established:** He has established a good reputation. (Usne apni achi pehchan bana li hai.)
*   **Disestablish:** They decided to disestablish the old network. (Unhone purana network khatam karne ka faisla kiya.)
*   **Disestablish:** The policy will disestablish the monopoly. (Ye policy monopoly khatam kar degi.)
*   **Establishment:** The establishment of the lab took time. (Lab ko qaim karne mein waqt laga.)
*   **Establishment:** Many academic establishments use this software. (Bohat se ilmi idaray ye software use karte hain.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The team wants to **establish** a new network. (Team ek naya network banana chahti hai.)
2. This project **establishes** a strong foundation. (Ye project ek mazboot bunyaad rakhta hai.)
3. **Establishing** a secure password is vital. (Ek secure password banana bohot zaroori hai.)
4. The **established** method works perfectly. (Ye pehle se chala aa raha tareeqa bilkul theek kaam karta hai.)
5. We will **disestablish** the connection after testing. (Test karne ke baad hum connection khatam kar denge.)
6. The research **establishment** published the report. (Research idaray ne report shaya ki.)
7. We need to **establish** trust between systems. (Hamein systems ke darmiyan bharosa qaim karna hoga.)
8. The company **establishes** new standards every year. (Company har saal naye standards banati hai.)
9. They are **establishing** a new database. (Wo ek naya database bana rahe hain.)
10. The **established** rules must be followed. (Jo rules pehle se bane hain unhein follow karna hoga.)
11. It is hard to **disestablish** a major organization. (Kisi bade idaray ko khatam karna mushkil hota hai.)
12. The **establishment** of the internet changed the world. (Internet ke qayam ne duniya badal di.)
13. These **establishments** provide free Wi-Fi. (Ye idaray free Wi-Fi dete hain.)
14. The software **establishes** a link automatically. (Software khud-ba-khud link bana leta hai.)
15. He is **establishing** his career in Data Science. (Wo Data Science mein apna career bana raha hai.)

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

**Core Meaning:** To start, create, or prove something.

| Word | Simple Meaning | CS / Academic Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Establish | Start / Create | To create a connection or rule. |
| Established | Already done | A system that is already working. |
| Establishment | An organization | A university or research lab. |
| Disestablish | To stop / End | Removing a status or old system. |

**Memory Trick:** Think of **"Establish"** as **"Start-a-base"**. When you establish something, you are putting down a base or foundation for it to stand on!
