### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Factor
    *   Factor in (phrasal verb)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Factor
    *   Factors (plural)
    *   Factorization (less common)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Factorial (math context)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   *None commonly used*

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Factor (Noun):** A part or something that influences a result.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Koi aisi cheez jo kisi nateeje (result) par asar daale.
*   **Factor (Verb):** To include something when making a decision or calculation.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ko calculation ya faislay mein shamil karna.
*   **Factor in (Phrasal Verb):** To think about something before making a plan.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Plan banate waqt kisi cheez ka khayal rakhna.
*   **Factors (Plural Noun):** More than one thing that causes an effect.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada cheezein jo kisi baat ki wajah banti hain.
*   **Factorial (Adjective):** Related to math or multiplication.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Math ya hisab kitab se juda hua.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Element:** (Koi hissa ya juz)
*   **Component:** (Kisi cheez ka hissa)
*   **Consider:** (Gaur karna – *for the verb form*)

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Factor (Noun):** Speed is a key **factor** in software performance.
    *   *R.U:* Software ki speed performance mein ek ahem juz (factor) hai.
*   **Factor (Verb):** We must **factor** the cost of servers into our budget.
    *   *R.U:* Hamein servers ka kharcha apne budget mein shamil karna chahiye.
*   **Factors (Plural):** Many **factors** affect the security of a network.
    *   *R.U:* Bahut si cheezein (factors) network ki security par asar daalti hain.
*   **Factor in:** You should **factor in** the time for testing the code.
    *   *R.U:* Aapko code test karne ka waqt pehle se shamil (factor in) kar lena chahiye.

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1.  Price is a major **factor** for buying new hardware. (Nayi hardware khareedne ke liye qeemat ek ahem factor hai.)
2.  Did you **factor in** the memory usage for this program? (Kya aapne is program ke liye memory usage ka khayal rakha?)
3.  Several **factors** lead to system crashes. (Bahut se factors system crash hone ki wajah bante hain.)
4.  We need to **factor** these errors into our report. (Hamein in ghaltiyon ko apni report mein shamil karna hoga.)
5.  Time is a critical **factor** in research projects. (Research projects mein waqt ek bahut ahem factor hai.)
6.  The team failed to **factor in** the user feedback. (Team user feedback ko shamil karna bhool gayi.)
7.  Security is a **factor** that we cannot ignore. (Security ek aisa factor hai jise hum ignore nahi kar sakte.)
8.  There are many **factors** that make an algorithm efficient. (Bahut se factors hain jo algorithm ko behtar banate hain.)
9.  Please **factor in** the power consumption of the device. (Barah-e-karam device ki bijli ki khapat (power consumption) ko shamil karein.)
10. The **factor** of human error is always present. (Insani ghalti ka factor hamesha mojood rehta hai.)
11. **Factors** like heat can damage a computer. (Garmi jaise factors computer ko nuqsan pohancha sakte hain.)
12. We must **factor** the cost of maintenance into the plan. (Hamein maintenance ke kharche ko plan mein shamil karna hoga.)
13. Success depends on many **factors**. (Kamyabi bahut se factors par depend karti hai.)
14. Don't forget to **factor in** the software updates. (Software updates ko shamil karna mat bhoolna.)
15. This **factor** changed the entire research result. (Is factor ne poore research ke nateeje ko badal diya.)
