Hello! I am your English instructor. Today, we will learn about the word **"Impact."** This is a very important word in Computer Science and academic research. Let’s learn it together!

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:** impact, impacts, impacted, impacting
*   **Noun forms:** impact, impacts
*   **Adjective forms:** impactful (less common)
*   **Adverb forms:** None

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Impact (Noun):** A strong effect or change on someone or something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ka doosri cheez par gehra asar hona.
*   **Impact (Verb):** To have a strong effect on something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Asar daalna ya change laana.
*   **Impacts (Verb/Noun):** The plural form or third-person singular form.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Bahut saare asraat (noun) ya asar daalta hai (verb).
*   **Impacted (Verb):** Past tense of impact (happened in the past).
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Asar dala ya asar hua.
*   **Impacting (Verb):** Happening right now.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Asar daal raha hai.
*   **Impactful (Adjective):** Something that has a big, positive effect.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh cheez jo bara aur acha asar chhore.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

1.  **Effect (Noun):** Result or consequence. (*Roman Urdu: Nateeja ya asar.*)
2.  **Influence (Verb/Noun):** To change how something develops. (*Roman Urdu: Kisi cheez ko badalne ki taqat.*)
3.  **Affect (Verb):** To produce a change. (*Roman Urdu: Asar karna.*)

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Impact (Noun):** The impact of AI on coding is huge.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* AI ka coding par bahut bara asar hai.
*   **Impact (Verb):** New updates will impact the system speed.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Naye updates system ki speed par asar daalenge.
*   **Impacts (Noun):** We studied the negative impacts of bugs.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Humne bugs ke manfi (negative) asraat ka mutala kiya.
*   **Impacts (Verb):** Low memory impacts performance.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kam memory performance par asar daalti hai.
*   **Impacted (Verb):** The server error impacted our database.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Server error ne hamari database par asar dala.
*   **Impacted (Verb):** Many users were impacted by the outage.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Bahut saare users outage (service band hone) se mutasir hue.
*   **Impacting (Verb):** The virus is impacting the network security.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Virus network security par asar daal raha hai.
*   **Impacting (Verb):** New laws are impacting tech research.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Naye qawaneen tech research par asar daal rahe hain.

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The impact of cloud computing is visible in business. (Cloud computing ka asar business mein nazar aata hai.)
2. Poor code quality impacts software stability. (Kharab code software ki stability par asar daalta hai.)
3. How does this algorithm impact data privacy? (Yeh algorithm data privacy par kaise asar daalta hai?)
4. The researchers analyzed the impacts of the new update. (Researchers ne naye update ke asraat ka jaiza liya.)
5. Did the hardware change impact the output? (Kya hardware change ne output par asar dala?)
6. We are impacting the way people use computers. (Hum logon ke computer istemal karne ke tareeqe par asar daal rahe hain.)
7. The study showed impactful results for developers. (Study ne developers ke liye bahut ache nataij dikhaye.)
8. Cyber attacks are impacting global security. (Cyber attacks global security par asar daal rahe hain.)
9. The impact of 5G is very significant. (5G ka asar bahut ahem hai.)
10. Low battery life impacted the robot's movement. (Kam battery life ne robot ki movement par asar dala.)
11. Big data impacts how we make decisions. (Big data hamare faislon par asar daalta hai.)
12. The team discussed the impacts of AI ethics. (Team ne AI ethics ke asraat par baat ki.)
13. Is your project impacting the environment? (Kya tumhara project mahol par asar daal raha hai?)
14. The crash impacted all connected devices. (Crash ne sabhi connected devices par asar dala.)
15. This research has a positive impact on technology. (Is research ka technology par musbat asar hai.)

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

| Word | Simple Meaning | CS / Academic Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Impact** | Strong effect | Used to show how one thing changes another. |
| **Impacted** | Changed/Affected | Describing a past result on a system. |
| **Impacting** | Changing/Affecting | Describing a process currently in action. |
| **Impacts** | Multiple effects | Used when talking about many consequences. |

**Memory Trick:** Think of a **meteor** hitting the ground. It makes a big **"Impact"** (crater). In the same way, a bug **"impacts"** your code by creating a problem!
