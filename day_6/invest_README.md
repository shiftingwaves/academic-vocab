### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Invest (Verb):** To put money, time, or effort into something to get a good result later.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez mein paisa, waqt ya mehnat lagana taake baad mein faida ho.
*   **Invests (Verb):** Present tense for "he/she/it".
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh invest karta hai.
*   **Invested (Verb/Adj):** Past tense or feeling committed to something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Paisa laga chuke hain ya kisi kaam mein poori tarah shamil hain.
*   **Investing (Verb):** The act of putting money/time into something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Paisa ya waqt laga rahe hain.
*   **Reinvest (Verb):** To invest the profit again.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo munafa (profit) hua, usay dobara kisi kaam mein laga dena.
*   **Investment (Noun):** The money or time you put into a project.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh raqam ya waqt jo aap kisi project mein lagate hain.
*   **Investor (Noun):** A person or company that gives money.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh shakhs jo paisa lagata hai.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Invest:**
    1. **Fund:** Paisa dena ya finance karna.
    2. **Allocate:** Kisi khaas kaam ke liye resource ya paisa rakhna.
*   **Investment:**
    1. **Capital:** Woh raqam jo business mein lagayi jaye.
    2. **Contribution:** Kisi kaam mein apna hissa ya madad dalna.

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Invest:** We must invest in new AI technology. (Hamein nayi AI technology mein invest karna chahiye.)
*   **Invests:** The company invests heavily in research. (Company research mein bohat paisa lagati hai.)
*   **Invested:** They invested millions in software development. (Unhon ne software development mein millions lagaye.)
*   **Investing:** Investing in cybersecurity is important for data safety. (Cybersecurity mein invest karna data ki hifazat ke liye zaroori hai.)
*   **Reinvest:** The startup will reinvest its profits. (Startup apne munafay ko dobara invest karegi.)
*   **Investment:** This project requires a huge investment. (Is project ke liye bari investment chahiye.)
*   **Investor:** The investor liked our new app. (Investor ko hamari nayi app pasand aayi.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The university decided to **invest** in high-speed servers. (University ne high-speed servers mein invest karne ka faisla kiya.)
2. Many **investors** are looking for AI startups. (Bohat se investors AI startups dhoond rahe hain.)
3. The **investment** in cloud computing was successful. (Cloud computing mein ki gayi investment kamyab rahi.)
4. She is **investing** her time in learning Python. (Woh apna waqt Python seekhne mein laga rahi hai.)
5. The firm **reinvests** its earnings into new hardware. (Firm apni kamayi ko naye hardware mein dobara invest karti hai.)
6. He is a very smart **investor**. (Woh ek bohat samajhdar investor hai.)
7. We have **invested** enough resources in this project. (Hum is project mein kaafi resources laga chuke hain.)
8. **Reinvesting** profit helps a company grow faster. (Munafay ko dobara invest karna company ko tezi se barhne mein madad deta hai.)
9. Total **investments** in tech reached a record high. (Tech mein kul investments record satah tak pahunch gayin.)
10. The researchers **invested** weeks into data cleaning. (Researchers ne data cleaning mein hafte lagaye.)
11. Are you planning on **reinvesting** your dividends? (Kya aap apne dividends dobara invest karne ka irada rakhte hain?)
12. This **investment** will improve our network speed. (Yeh investment hamari network speed ko behtar banayegi.)
13. The **investor** asked for a demo of the software. (Investor ne software ka demo manga.)
14. They **reinvested** the grant money into lab equipment. (Unhon ne grant ka paisa lab ke equipment mein dobara lagaya.)
15. Smart **investing** is key to academic success. (Samajhdari se invest karna academic kamyabi ki chabi hai.)
