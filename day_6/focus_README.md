### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Focus (Verb):** To give all your attention to one thing.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi ek cheez par poori tawajo dena.
*   **Focus (Noun):** The center of interest or activity.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh cheez jis par sab ki nazar ya tawajo ho.
*   **Focused (Adjective):** Giving a lot of attention to a goal.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jab aap kisi kaam par puri tarah concentrate kar rahe hon.
*   **Refocus (Verb):** To focus again on something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Dobara tawajo dena ya nayi tarah se concentrate karna.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Concentrate:** Tawajo markooz karna.
*   **Center:** Markaz banana.
*   **Target:** Kisi maqsad par nazar rakhna.

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

1.  **Focus:** Researchers must focus on the security of the new software.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Researchers ko naye software ki security par tawajo deni chahiye.
2.  **Focuses:** The article focuses on Artificial Intelligence.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Yeh article Artificial Intelligence par tawajo deta hai.
3.  **Focused:** The team is focused on completing the code.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Team code mukammal karne par focused hai.
4.  **Refocus:** We need to refocus our research on user privacy.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Hamein apni research ko dobara user privacy par focus karne ki zaroorat hai.

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1.  The study focuses on data encryption. (Study data encryption par tawajo deti hai.)
2.  Stay focused while debugging the program. (Program debug karte waqt focused rahen.)
3.  We must refocus our efforts to fix the bug. (Hamein bug theek karne ke liye apni koshishein dobara markooz karni hongi.)
4.  His focus is on machine learning. (Us ki tawajo ka markaz machine learning hai.)
5.  The team is focusing on efficiency. (Team efficiency par tawajo de rahi hai.)
6.  The new update focuses on speed. (Naya update speed par tawajo deta hai.)
7.  I am focused on my final project. (Main apne final project par focused hoon.)
8.  Let us refocus on the main problem. (Chalo hum dobara asal maslay par tawajo dete hain.)
9.  This lab focuses on cyber security. (Yeh lab cyber security par tawajo deti hai.)
10. A focused mind learns coding faster. (Ek focused dimaag coding jaldi seekhta hai.)
11. The professor refocused the lecture on algorithms. (Professor ne lecture ko dobara algorithms par shift kiya.)
12. Our main focus is user experience. (Hamari bunyadi tawajo user experience hai.)
13. She is focusing on the database design. (Woh database design par tawajo de rahi hai.)
14. The company refocused its business model. (Company ne apne business model par dobara tawajo di.)
15. Keep your focus on the screen. (Apni tawajo screen par rakhen.)
