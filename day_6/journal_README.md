Hello! I am your English language instructor. Today, we will learn about the word **"Journal"**. This word is very important when you read or write academic papers in Computer Science.

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Journal (to write in a journal)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Journal (the main word)
    *   Journals (plural form)
    *   Journalism (the activity of reporting news)
    *   Journalist (a person who writes news)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Journalistic (related to writing news)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   (None commonly used)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Journal (Noun):** A book or magazine where experts write about their research.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ye ek aisi kitab ya magazine hoti hai jisme experts apni research ya nayi khoj likhte hain.
*   **Journals (Noun - Plural):** More than one journal.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada journals.
*   **Journal (Verb):** To keep a record of your work or thoughts daily.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Rozana apne kaam ya khayalat ko likhna.
*   **Journalism (Noun):** The work of collecting and writing news.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Khabrein ikatha karna aur unhein likhna.
*   **Journalist (Noun):** A person who writes news.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Wo shakhs jo khabrein likhta hai.
*   **Journalistic (Adjective):** Something that is like news writing.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo cheez news writing jaisi ho.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Periodical:** A magazine published at regular times. (Roman Urdu: Wo risala jo waqfay waqfay se chhaype).
*   **Publication:** A document made to be read by the public. (Roman Urdu: Koi bhi chhaypi hui cheez).
*   **Log:** A record of events. (Roman Urdu: Waqiyat ka record).

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

**Journal (Noun)**
1. This computer science journal publishes new AI research. (Ye CS journal nayi AI research shaya karta hai.)
2. I found a great article in the IEEE journal. (Mujhe IEEE journal mein ek behtareen article mila.)

**Journals (Noun)**
1. Many online journals are free for students. (Bahut sare online journals students ke liye free hain.)
2. We searched three journals for information on coding. (Humne coding ki maloomat ke liye teen journals search kiye.)

**Journal (Verb)**
1. Researchers journal their daily progress in the lab. (Researchers lab mein apne rozana ke kaam ko record karte hain.)
2. You should journal your coding challenges to learn faster. (Aapko apne coding challenges ko likhna chahiye taake aap jaldi seekh saken.)

**Journalism/Journalist/Journalistic**
1. Data journalism helps explain complex tech news. (Data journalism mushkil tech khabron ko samajhne mein madad karti hai.)
2. The journalist interviewed the software engineer. (Journalist ne software engineer ka interview liya.)
3. The report had a very journalistic style. (Report ka andaz bilkul news jaisa tha.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The journal focuses on cybersecurity trends. (Ye journal cybersecurity ke trends par focus karta hai.)
2. Many academic journals require peer reviews. (Bahut se academic journals peer review mangte hain.)
3. Please check the latest journals for new algorithms. (Naye algorithms ke liye latest journals check karein.)
4. I like to journal my experiments every evening. (Main har sham apne experiments ko record karta hoon.)
5. The journalist wrote about the new smartphone launch. (Journalist ne naye smartphone launch ke bare mein likha.)
6. High-quality journals are important for research. (Achhe journals research ke liye zaroori hain.)
7. The journalistic approach made the tech news easy to read. (Journalistic approach ne tech news ko parhna asaan bana diya.)
8. Does your university provide access to these journals? (Kya aapki university in journals ka access deti hai?)
9. He is a famous journalist in the technology field. (Wo technology ke field mein ek mashhoor journalist hai.)
10. We must cite the journal in our paper. (Hamein apne paper mein journal ka hawala dena hoga.)
11. Reading journals helps you stay updated. (Journals parhne se aap updated rehte hain.)
12. The student decided to journal his project steps. (Student ne apne project ke steps ko likhne ka faisla kiya.)
13. That journal is famous for its AI articles. (Wo journal apne AI articles ke liye mashhoor hai.)
14. Good journalism requires checking all facts. (Achhi journalism ke liye tamam facts check karna zaroori hai.)
15. I found this paper in a computer science journal. (Mujhe ye paper ek computer science journal mein mila.)
