### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Design
    *   Redesign (to design again)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Design
    *   Designer / Designers
    *   Design (as a concept)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Designed
    *   Designing (often used as an adjective, e.g., "designing phase")
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   *None commonly used in academic writing.*

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Design (Verb):** To plan or create how something will look or work.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ka naksha banana ya planning karna ke woh kaisi dikhay gi ya kaise kaam kare gi.
*   **Redesign (Verb):** To plan or create something again to make it better.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ko dobara behtar banane ke liye plan karna.
*   **Design (Noun):** The plan or drawing of something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ka naksha ya planning.
*   **Designer (Noun):** A person who creates the plan for something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh shakhs jo naksha ya planning banata hai.
*   **Designed (Adjective):** Something that has a specific plan.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh cheez jis ka pehle se ek khaas naksha banaya gaya ho.
*   **Designing (Adjective/Verb):** The process of creating a plan.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Planning ya naksha banane ka amal.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

1.  **Plan:** (Ek mansooba ya naksha banana)
2.  **Create:** (Koi nayi cheez banana)
3.  **Formulate:** (Kisi cheez ko tarteeb dena)

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Design (Verb):** We need to **design** a new database for this project.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Humein is project ke liye ek naya database design karne ki zaroorat hai.
*   **Redesign (Verb):** The team decided to **redesign** the website interface.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Team ne website ka interface dobara design karne ka faisla kiya.
*   **Design (Noun):** The **design** of this algorithm is very efficient.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Is algorithm ka design bohat efficient (kaam karne wala) hai.
*   **Designer (Noun):** The software **designer** fixed the bug in the code.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Software designer ne code mein se ghalti (bug) theek kar di.
*   **Designed (Adjective):** The system is **designed** to handle many users at once.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Yeh system is tarah design kiya gaya hai ke woh ek waqt mein bohat se users ko handle kar sake.
*   **Designing (Adjective):** The **designing** phase took three months to complete.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Designing ka marhala (phase) mukammal hone mein teen mahine lage.

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The **design** of the new chip is impressive. (Naye chip ka design bohat zabardast hai.)
2. We must **design** a secure network. (Humein ek mehfooz network design karna chahiye.)
3. The **designer** used a new tool. (Designer ne ek naya tool istemaal kiya.)
4. The system was **designed** for research. (System research ke liye design kiya gaya tha.)
5. **Designing** software requires patience. (Software design karne ke liye sabar ki zaroorat hoti hai.)
6. They will **redesign** the app. (Woh app ko dobara design karenge.)
7. Good **design** helps the user. (Acha design user ki madad karta hai.)
8. The **designer** finished the task. (Designer ne kaam khatam kar liya.)
9. The **designed** model is accurate. (Jo model design kiya gaya hai woh durust hai.)
10. We are **designing** a robot. (Hum ek robot design kar rahe hain.)
11. The **design** meets all requirements. (Yeh design tamam zarooriyaat ko poora karta hai.)
12. The **designer** works in a lab. (Designer lab mein kaam karta hai.)
13. The **designed** layout is clear. (Jo layout design kiya gaya hai woh saaf hai.)
14. **Designing** is a creative process. (Designing ek creative kaam hai.)
15. He will **redesign** the database schema. (Woh database schema ko dobara design karega.)

| **Design** | Plan/Create | Used for software/hardware structure |
