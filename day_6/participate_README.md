### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Participate
    *   Participated (past tense)
    *   Participates (present, third person)
    *   Participating (present participle)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Participant (a person)
    *   Participants (plural people)
    *   Participation (the act of taking part)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Participatory (involving people)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   *(None common in academic use)*

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Participate (Verb):** To be part of an activity.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi kaam ya activity ka hissa banna ya shamil hona.
*   **Participant (Noun):** A person who takes part in something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh shakhs jo kisi activity mein hissa leta hai.
*   **Participation (Noun):** The action of taking part.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Hissa lene ka amal (involvement).
*   **Participatory (Adjective):** Something that lets many people join in.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Aisi cheez jismein sab log mil kar hissa le saken.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Take part:** Hissa lena.
*   **Engage:** Masroof hona ya shamil hona.
*   **Involve:** Shamil karna.

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Participate:** 
    1. Students must participate in the lab session. (Students ko lab session mein hissa lena chahiye.)
    2. Many researchers participate in global conferences. (Bohat se researchers global conferences mein shamil hote hain.)
*   **Participant:** 
    1. Each participant received a survey form. (Har participant ko survey form mila.)
    2. The participants tested the new software. (Participants ne naya software test kiya.)
*   **Participation:** 
    1. Active participation is required for this course. (Is course ke liye active participation zaroori hai.)
    2. The study measured user participation. (Study ne user ki participation ko measure kiya.)
*   **Participatory:** 
    1. It was a participatory design workshop. (Yeh ek participatory design workshop tha.)
    2. The system uses a participatory approach. (System ek participatory approach use karta hai.)

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

**Core Meaning:** To join or be part of a group activity.

| Word | Simple Meaning | CS / Academic Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Participate** | Hissa lena | Joining a project or study. |
| **Participant** | Hissa lene wala | A person in a research study. |
