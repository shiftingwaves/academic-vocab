### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Major (in) – (to choose a subject of study)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Major (a student's main subject)
    *   Majority (the larger part or number)
    *   Majorities (plural of majority)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Major (very important or large)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   (None commonly used in academic contexts)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Major (Verb):** To study a subject as your main focus.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi khas subject ko apni parhai ka main hissa banana.
*   **Major (Noun):** The main subject you are studying at university.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Aap ka main field ya subject jise aap seekh rahe hain.
*   **Majority (Noun):** More than half of a group.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek group ka sab se bada hissa ya taadad (aadhi se zyada).
*   **Majorities (Noun - Plural):** Many groups that are more than half.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jab ek se zyada groups mein majority ki baat ho.
*   **Major (Adjective):** Very big, important, or serious.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Bohat bara, ahem, ya zaroori.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Major (Adjective):**
    *   **Significant:** Kafi ahem ya zaroori.
    *   **Main:** Sab se bara ya markazi.
    *   **Primary:** Pehla ya sab se zaroori.

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Major (Verb):**
    *   He decided to major in Computer Science. (Usne Computer Science mein major karne ka faisla kiya.)
    *   Many students major in Data Science these days. (Aaj kal bohat se talib-e-ilm Data Science mein major karte hain.)
*   **Major (Noun):**
    *   Her major is Artificial Intelligence. (Uska major Artificial Intelligence hai.)
    *   Is your major related to software engineering? (Kya aapka major software engineering se related hai?)
*   **Majority:**
    *   The majority of students prefer Python for coding. (Talib-e-ilmon ki aksariyat coding ke liye Python pasand karti hai.)
    *   A majority of users reported a bug in the app. (Users ki aksariyat ne app mein ek bug report kiya.)
*   **Major (Adjective):**
    *   This is a major issue in the system security. (Yeh system security mein ek bara masla hai.)
    *   The project requires a major change in the code. (Project ko code mein ek bari tabdeeli ki zaroorat hai.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1.  A **major** update was released for the operating system. (Operating system ke liye ek bari update jari ki gayi.)
2.  The **majority** of servers are running on Linux. (Zyadatar servers Linux par chal rahe hain.)
3.  She wants to **major** in Cybersecurity. (Woh Cybersecurity mein major karna chahti hai.)
4.  Data security is a **major** concern for companies. (Data security companiyon ke liye ek bara masla hai.)
5.  The **majority** of participants finished the test on time. (Hissa lene walon ki aksariyat ne test waqt par khatam kar liya.)
6.  This software has a **major** flaw. (Is software mein ek bari khami hai.)
7.  His **major** in university was Web Development. (University mein uska major Web Development tha.)
8.  The **majority** of errors were fixed by the team. (Team ne zyadatar galtiyan theek kar di hain.)
9.  We need a **major** shift in our research strategy. (Hamein apni research strategy mein bari tabdeeli ki zaroorat hai.)
10. A **majority** of developers use GitHub. (Developers ki aksariyat GitHub istemal karti hai.)
11. Choosing a **major** is a big decision. (Major chunna ek bara faisla hai.)
12. The **major** goal of this paper is to explain AI. (Is paper ka bara maqsad AI ko samjhana hai.)
13. The **majority** of the data is stored in the cloud. (Zyadatar data cloud mein store hota hai.)
14. This is a **major** breakthrough in quantum computing. (Yeh quantum computing mein ek bari kamyabi hai.)
15. He will **major** in Networking next semester. (Woh agle semester mein Networking mein major karega.)
