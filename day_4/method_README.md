### 2. EXPLANATION (FOR EACH WORD)

*   **Method:** A specific way of doing something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi kaam ko karne ka aik khaas tareeqa.
*   **Methods:** More than one way of doing something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kaam karne ke mukhtalif tareeqay.
*   **Methodology:** The study or the system of methods used in research.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Research karne ka poora system ya tareeqa-e-kaar.
*   **Methodologies:** More than one system of research.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Research ke mukhtalif systems ya tareeqa-e-kaar.
*   **Methodical:** Doing things in a very organized and careful way.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Har kaam ko tarteeb aur sukoon se karna.
*   **Methodological:** Related to the system or method of research.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Research ke tareeqay se mutaliq.
*   **Methodically:** Doing something in a careful and organized manner.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Tarteeb se ya nizaam ke saath kaam karna.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Method (Noun):**
    *   *Technique:* Kaam karne ki technique/skill.
    *   *Approach:* Kisi maslay ko hal karne ka raasta.
    *   *Procedure:* Step-by-step tareeqa.

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Method:**
    *   We used a new method to test the software. (Humne software test karne ke liye aik naya tareeqa istemal kiya.)
    *   This encryption method is very secure. (Yeh encryption ka tareeqa bohat mehfooz hai.)
*   **Methods:**
    *   The paper compares two different methods. (Yeh paper do mukhtalif tareeqon ka mawazna karta hai.)
    *   We need better methods for data analysis. (Hamein data analysis ke liye behtar tareeqon ki zaroorat hai.)
*   **Methodology:**
    *   Our methodology follows standard guidelines. (Hamara tareeqa-e-kaar standard usoolon ko follow karta hai.)
    *   The methodology section explains how the experiment was done. (Methodology ka hissa batata hai ke experiment kaise hua.)
*   **Methodologies:**
    *   The researchers used several methodologies. (Researchers ne bohat si methodologies istemal keen.)
    *   Different methodologies give different results. (Mukhtalif tareeqa-e-kaar mukhtalif nateeje dete hain.)
*   **Methodical:**
    *   A methodical approach is important for coding. (Coding ke liye aik tarteebi approach zaroori hai.)
    *   She is very methodical in her research. (Woh apni research mein bohat tarteeb se kaam karti hai.)
*   **Methodological:**
    *   There is a methodological error in this study. (Is study mein tareeqay se mutaliq ghalti hai.)
    *   The report discusses methodological issues. (Report mein tareeqay se juri masail par baat hui hai.)
*   **Methodically:**
    *   The team methodically checked every line of code. (Team ne har line ko tarteeb se check kiya.)
    *   He methodically organized the database. (Usne database ko nizaam ke saath tarteeb diya.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The research team chose a new **method** for data collection. (Research team ne data jama karne ke liye naya tareeqa chuna.)
2. It is essential to be **methodical** when debugging large programs. (Barray programs debug karte waqt tarteeb se kaam karna zaroori hai.)
3. Our **methodology** ensures that the results are accurate. (Hamara tareeqa-e-kaar yaqeeni banata hai ke nateeje theek hon.)
4. The **methods** used in this paper are outdated. (Is paper mein istemal hone wale tareeqay purane ho chuke hain.)
5. We **methodically** analyzed the system logs. (Humne system logs ko tarteeb se analyze kiya.)
6. The **methodological** framework is clearly explained. (Tareeqay ka dhancha wazeh tor par samjhaya gaya hai.)
7. Many software **methodologies** exist for project management. (Project management ke liye bohat si methodologies mojood hain.)
8. His **methodical** way of working saves a lot of time. (Uska tarteeb se kaam karne ka andaz bohat waqt bachata hai.)
9. The study uses a **method** called "Random Sampling." (Study "Random Sampling" naam ka tareeqa istemal karti hai.)
10. **Methodological** rigor is required for a PhD thesis. (PhD thesis ke liye tareeqay ki sakhti se pabandi zaroori hai.)
11. We need to update our current **methodology**. (Hamein apne mojooda tareeqa-e-kaar ko update karne ki zaroorat hai.)
12. The programmer **methodically** fixed the security bugs. (Programmer ne security bugs ko tarteeb se theek kiya.)
13. Several **methods** were tested to optimize the algorithm. (Algorithm ko optimize karne ke liye kai tareeqay test kiye gaye.)
14. The **methodological** approach was approved by the professor. (Professor ne tareeqay ko manzoor kar liya.)
15. A **methodical** search helped find the error. (Aik tarteebi talash ne ghalti dhoondne mein madad ki.)

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

| Word | Simple Meaning | Common CS Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Method** | Way / Procedure | Function inside a class |
| **Methodology** | System of doing | Research process |
| **Methodical** | Organized | Careful coding/testing |

**Memory Trick:**
*   **Method** = "Me-thod" (My way of doing it).
*   **Methodical** = "Method + I + Call" (I call for a very organized way). 
*   *Think of "Method" as a "Function" in programming—it is just a way to do a specific task!*
