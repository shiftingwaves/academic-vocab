Hello! I am your English language instructor. Today, we will learn about the word family **"Period."** This is a very important word in academic writing and Computer Science. Let’s start!

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Period (less common)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Period
    *   Periodicals (plural)
    *   Periods (plural)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Periodic
    *   Periodical
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   Periodically

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Period (Noun):** A length of time.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek waqt ka hissa ya time frame.
*   **Periods (Noun):** More than one length of time.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada waqt ke hissay.
*   **Periodical (Adjective/Noun):** Something that happens from time to time; a magazine published regularly.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo kabhi kabhi ho; ya woh magazine jo rozana ya haftawar chapti hai.
*   **Periodicals (Noun):** Magazines or journals published regularly.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Wo magazines ya journals jo ek khas waqt ke baad chaptay hain.
*   **Periodic (Adjective):** Happening again and again at fixed times.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo ek khas waqt ke baad baar baar ho.
*   **Periodically (Adverb):** Doing something from time to time.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kabhi kabhi, ya ek khas waqt ke waqfay se.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Period:**
    *   *Phase:* Ek khas daur.
    *   *Interval:* Do cheezon ke darmiyan ka waqt.
*   **Periodic:**
    *   *Recurrent:* Jo baar baar ho.
    *   *Cyclic:* Jo ek cycle mein ho.

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Period:**
    1. The testing **period** for the new software is two weeks. (Software ki testing ka waqt do haftay hai.)
    2. During this **period**, the computer system was very slow. (Is waqt ke dauran, computer system bohat slow tha.)
*   **Periods:**
    1. Researchers studied data over several **periods**. (Researchers ne bohat se waqt ke hisson mein data study kiya.)
    2. The project was divided into three short **periods**. (Project ko teen chotay hisson mein taqseem kiya gaya.)
*   **Periodical (Adj/Noun):**
    1. This **periodical** journal discusses new AI trends. (Ye journal nayi AI trends par baat karta hai.)
    2. She reads a scientific **periodical** every month. (Wo har mahinay ek scientific magazine parhti hai.)
*   **Periodicals:**
    1. The library has many computer science **periodicals**. (Library mein computer science ki bohat si magazines hain.)
    2. We use online **periodicals** for our research. (Hum apni research ke liye online journals use karte hain.)
*   **Periodic:**
    1. The server needs **periodic** maintenance to work well. (Server ko sahi chalne ke liye waqt-waqt par maintenance chahiye.)
    2. **Periodic** updates keep the system safe from hackers. (Waqt-waqt par honay wali updates system ko hackers se mehfooz rakhti hain.)
*   **Periodically:**
    1. You should back up your files **periodically**. (Aap ko apne files waqt-waqt par save karni chahiye.)
    2. The software checks for new updates **periodically**. (Software khud se waqt-waqt par updates check karta hai.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The research **period** lasted for six months. (Research ka waqt chhay mahinay raha.)
2. **Periodic** system checks prevent crashes. (System ki checking crashes se bachati hai.)
3. I read technical **periodicals** to learn coding. (Main coding seekhne ke liye technical magazines parhta hoon.)
4. Data is backed up **periodically** to avoid loss. (Data loss se bachne ke liye waqt-waqt par save kiya jata hai.)
5. Three **periods** of testing were required. (Testing ke teen hissay zaroori thay.)
6. This **periodical** covers the latest in web development. (Ye magazine web development ki nayi cheezon ke bare mein hai.)
7. **Periodic** signals are used in digital communication. (Digital communication mein periodic signals use hote hain.)
8. The library stores old **periodicals** for students. (Library students ke liye purani magazines rakhti hai.)
9. During the final **period**, we fixed all bugs. (Aakhri waqt mein, hum ne saare bugs theek kar diye.)
10. The system **periodically** logs user activity. (System waqt-waqt par user ki activity ko record karta hai.)
11. Many **periodicals** are now available in digital format. (Bohat si magazines ab digital format mein milti hain.)
12. **Periodic** reviews help improve code quality. (Waqt-waqt par reviews code ki quality behtar karte hain.)
13. The project was completed in a short **period**. (Project kam waqt mein mukammal ho gaya.)
14. Search for articles in academic **periodicals**. (Academic journals mein articles dhundhein.)
15. The network connection was lost for a short **period**. (Kuch waqt ke liye network connection khatam ho gaya tha.)
