### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

    *   Research

    *   Research

    *   Researchers (Plural)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Research-based (Commonly used in academic contexts)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   *(None commonly used)*

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Research (Verb):** To study a subject carefully to find new facts.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi topic ke bare mein gehri study karna taake naye facts ya information mile.
*   **Researches (Verb):** He/She/It studies a subject carefully.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jab koi akela shakhs ya machine kisi cheez ko study karti hai.
*   **Researched (Verb):** Studied a subject in the past.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo kaam pehle study kiya ja chuka ho.
*   **Researching (Verb):** The action of studying a subject right now.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Abhi study karne ka amal.
*   **Research (Noun):** The work of finding new information.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh kaam ya study jo naye ilm (knowledge) ke liye ki jaye.
*   **Researcher (Noun):** A person who does research.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh shakhs jo research karta hai (a scientist or student).
*   **Researchers (Noun):** More than one person who does research.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada log jo research karte hain.
*   **Research-based (Adjective):** Something that is built on facts from research.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Aisi cheez jo research ki bunyad par bani ho.

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Research (Verb):** We need to **research** new AI algorithms. (Hamein naye AI algorithms par research karne ki zaroorat hai.)
*   **Research (Noun):** The **research** shows that the system is fast. (Research dikhati hai ke system fast hai.)
*   **Researches (Verb):** She **researches** network security daily. (Woh rozana network security par research karti hai.)
*   **Researched (Verb):** They **researched** the data for two years. (Unhon ne do saal tak data par research ki.)
*   **Researching (Verb):** He is **researching** cloud computing. (Woh cloud computing par research kar raha hai.)
*   **Researcher (Noun):** The **researcher** found a new bug. (Researcher ne ek naya bug dhoond nikala.)
*   **Researchers (Noun):** **Researchers** are testing the code. (Researchers code ko test kar rahe hain.)
*   **Research-based (Adj):** This is a **research-based** project. (Yeh ek research par mabni project hai.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The team is **researching** better ways to store data. (Team data store karne ke behtar tareeqon par research kar rahi hai.)
2. Many **researchers** work in this lab. (Is lab mein bohot se researchers kaam karte hain.)
3. I have **researched** this topic for my paper. (Maine apne paper ke liye is topic par research ki hai.)
4. The **research** was very helpful for our CS project. (Yeh research hamare CS project ke liye bohot madadgar thi.)
5. Every **researcher** needs a good laptop. (Har researcher ko ek ache laptop ki zaroorat hoti hai.)
6. She **researches** how robots learn to walk. (Woh research karti hai ke robots chalna kaise seekhte hain.)
7. We need **research-based** evidence for this claim. (Hamein is daaway ke liye research-based saboot chahiye.)
8. The **researching** process takes a lot of time. (Research karne ka amal bohot waqt leta hai.)
9. Top **researchers** presented their findings today. (Top researchers ne aaj apni findings pesh ki.)
10. Can you **research** the history of Java? (Kya tum Java ki history par research kar sakte ho?)
11. His **research** changed how we see software. (Us ki research ne software ko dekhne ka nazariya badal diya.)
12. Are you **researching** machine learning? (Kya tum machine learning par research kar rahe ho?)
13. The **researcher** published a new article. (Researcher ne ek naya article publish kiya.)
14. This **research-based** software is very stable. (Yeh research-based software bohot stable hai.)
15. Our university **researches** green technology. (Hamari university green technology par research karti hai.)
