Hello! I am your English language instructor. Today, we will learn about the word family: **Percent, Percentage, Percentages**. These words are very important in Computer Science and research, especially when we talk about data and statistics.

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:** None (N/A)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Percent
    *   Percentage
    *   Percentages
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Percent (used as an adjective, e.g., "a 50 percent increase")
*   **Adverb forms:** None (N/A)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Percent:** A part of 100.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Yeh 100 mein se koi hissa hota hai.
*   **Percentage:** A rate or number calculated out of 100.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Yeh woh value ya rate hai jo 100 ke hisaab se nikali jati hai.
*   **Percentages (Plural):** More than one percentage value.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jab hum aik se zyada percentages ki baat karte hain.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Proportion:** (Hissa ya ratio)
*   **Rate:** (Sharrah)
*   **Ratio:** (Do cheezon ka apas mein taluq)

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

**Percent**
1. The software error rate is only 2 percent. (Software mein ghalti ki sharrah sirf 2 percent hai.)
2. We achieved a 10 percent improvement in processing speed. (Hum ne processing speed mein 10 percent behtari hasil ki.)

**Percentage**
1. The percentage of successful logins increased today. (Aaj kamyab logins ki percentage barh gayi.)
2. We need to calculate the percentage of memory used. (Hamein istemal hone wali memory ki percentage calculate karni hai.)

**Percentages**
1. The researchers compared the percentages of different data sets. (Researchers ne mukhtalif data sets ki percentages ka mawazna kiya.)
2. These percentages show a decline in server performance. (Yeh percentages server ki karkardagi mein kami dikhati hain.)

---

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The total **percentage** of CPU usage was high. (CPU usage ki kul percentage zyada thi.)
2. Only 5 **percent** of the users reported a bug. (Sirf 5 percent users ne bug report kiya.)
3. We compared the **percentages** of two different algorithms. (Hum ne do mukhtalif algorithms ki percentages ka mawazna kiya.)
4. The **percentage** of accuracy is very important in AI models. (AI models mein accuracy ki percentage bohat ahem hai.)
5. A 20 **percent** increase in traffic crashed the website. (Traffic mein 20 percent izafay ne website ko crash kar diya.)
6. Please show the **percentages** in a pie chart. (Baraye meharbani percentages ko pie chart mein dikhayen.)
7. The **percentage** of battery life remaining is low. (Baqi rehne wali battery life ki percentage kam hai.)
8. We analyzed the **percentages** of code coverage. (Hum ne code coverage ki percentages ka tajziya kiya.)
9. A high **percentage** of students prefer online learning. (Students ki aik bari percentage online learning ko pasand karti hai.)
10. The **percent** change in data size is minimal. (Data size mein percent tabdeeli bohat kam hai.)
11. These **percentages** help us understand the user behavior. (Yeh percentages hamein user ka rawayya samajhne mein madad karti hain.)
12. The final **percentage** was rounded to two decimal places. (Aakhri percentage ko do decimal places tak round kiya gaya.)
13. Can you calculate the **percentage** of packet loss? (Kya aap packet loss ki percentage calculate kar sakte hain?)
14. The **percentages** vary across different operating systems. (Yeh percentages mukhtalif operating systems mein mukhtalif hain.)
15. A small **percent** of the data is missing. (Data ka aik chota sa hissa (percent) gayab hai.)

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

| Word | Simple Meaning | Common Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Percent** | Out of 100 | Used with numbers (e.g., 50 percent) |
| **Percentage** | A rate / portion | Used for general trends (e.g., the percentage of error) |
| **Percentages** | Multiple values | Used when comparing many results |

**Memory Trick:**
*   **Percent** is like a "part" of 100. Always put a number before it (e.g., 10 percent).
*   **Percentage** is the "name" of the idea. Use it when talking about the concept (e.g., "The percentage is high").
