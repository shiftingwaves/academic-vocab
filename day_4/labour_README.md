Hello! I am your English instructor. Today, we will learn about the word **"Labor"** (or **"Labour"**). 

*Note: "Labor" is American English spelling, and "Labour" is British English spelling. Both are correct!*

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:** labor, labour, labored, laboured, laboring, labouring, labors, labours.
*   **Noun forms:** labor, labour, laborer (less common), labourer (less common).
*   **Adjective forms:** laborious (hard/difficult).
*   **Adverb forms:** laboriously (in a hard/difficult way).

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Labor / Labour (Verb):** To work very hard. (Sakht mehnat karna).
*   **Labor / Labour (Noun):** Hard physical or mental work. (Mehnat ya kaam).
*   **Laborer / Labourer (Noun):** A person who does hard work. (Mehnat karne wala).
*   **Laborious (Adjective):** Something that takes a lot of time and effort. (Bohat mushkil ya mehnat talab kaam).
*   **Laboriously (Adverb):** Doing something with a lot of effort. (Mehnat ke sath).

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Work:** Kaam karna.
*   **Toil:** Sakht mehnat karna.
*   **Effort:** Koshish ya mehnat.

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Labor (Verb):** Researchers labor to find new solutions for AI errors.
    *(Researchers AI ki ghaltiyan theek karne ke liye sakht mehnat karte hain.)*
*   **Labor (Noun):** Developing complex software requires a lot of mental labor.
    *(Complex software banane ke liye bohat zehni mehnat ki zaroorat hoti hai.)*
*   **Laborious (Adjective):** Data entry is a laborious task for many students.
    *(Data entry bohat sare students ke liye mehnat talab kaam hai.)*
*   **Laboriously (Adverb):** The team laboriously cleaned the dataset for three weeks.
    *(Team ne teen hafte tak mehnat se dataset ko saaf kiya.)*

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The scientists labored for months on the new algorithm. (Scientists ne mahino naye algorithm par mehnat ki.)
2. Coding can be a laborious process for beginners. (Beginners ke liye coding ek mushkil aur mehnat talab kaam ho sakta hai.)
3. Manual labor in research is decreasing due to automation. (Research mein manual kaam automation ki wajah se kam ho raha hai.)
4. They laboriously tested every single line of code. (Unhon ne mehnat se code ki har line ko test kiya.)
5. Much labor is needed to complete this research project. (Is research project ko pura karne ke liye bohat mehnat chahiye.)
6. The programmer labored over the bug until it was fixed. (Programmer ne bug par tab tak mehnat ki jab tak woh theek nahi ho gaya.)
7. It was a laborious task to organize the files. (Files ko organize karna ek mehnat talab kaam tha.)
8. The lab team labored to meet the deadline. (Lab team ne deadline puri karne ke liye sakht mehnat ki.)
9. Automated systems reduce the labor of data processing. (Automated systems data processing ki mehnat ko kam karte hain.)
10. He laboriously typed the long report. (Usne mehnat se lamba report type kiya.)
11. Research involves the labor of many experts. (Research mein bohat se experts ki mehnat shamil hoti hai.)
12. The task proved to be quite laborious. (Yeh kaam kafi mehnat talab sabit hua.)
13. We labor to improve our system's security. (Hum apne system ki security ko behtar banane ke liye mehnat karte hain.)
14. The labor involved in this experiment was massive. (Is experiment mein shamil mehnat bohat zyada thi.)
15. She laboriously gathered information from different sources. (Usne mehnat se mukhtalif sources se maloomat ikathi ki.)

| :--- | :--- | :--- |

**Memory Trick:** Think of **"Labor"** as **"Long work"**. If you have to do something for a long time and it is tiring, it is "Laborious"!
