Hello! I am your English instructor. Today, we are going to learn about the word family **"Legislate"**. This word is very important in academic writing, especially when talking about laws and rules in technology.

Let’s start!

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:** Legislate, legislated, legislates, legislating.
*   **Noun forms:** Legislation, legislator, legislators, legislature.
*   **Adjective forms:** Legislative.
*   **Adverb forms:** *None commonly used in academic English.*

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Legislate (Verb):** To make a law. (Kanoon banana).
*   **Legislated (Verb - Past):** Made a law in the past. (Kanoon banaya).
*   **Legislates (Verb - Present):** Makes a law. (Kanoon banata hai).
*   **Legislating (Verb - Continuous):** The process of making a law. (Kanoon banane ka amal).
*   **Legislation (Noun):** A law or a set of laws. (Kanoon ya kanoon ka majmua).
*   **Legislator (Noun):** A person who makes laws. (Kanoon banane wala).
*   **Legislators (Noun - Plural):** People who make laws. (Kanoon banane wale).
*   **Legislature (Noun):** A group of people (like a parliament) that makes laws. (Woh idara ya group jo kanoon banata hai).
*   **Legislative (Adjective):** Related to making laws. (Kanoon saazi se mutaliq).

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Legislate:**
    *   **Enact:** Kanoon naafiz karna.
    *   **Pass:** Kanoon manzoor karna.
*   **Legislation:**
    *   **Statute:** Sarkari kanoon.
    *   **Law:** Kanoon.

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

1.  **Legislate:** Governments must **legislate** rules for Artificial Intelligence. (Hukumat ko AI ke liye kanoon banane chahiye.)
2.  **Legislate:** It is hard to **legislate** for fast-changing technology. (Tezi se badalti technology ke liye kanoon banana mushkil hai.)
3.  **Legislated:** The state **legislated** new privacy rules last year. (State ne pichle saal privacy ke naye kanoon banaye.)
4.  **Legislated:** Many countries have **legislated** against cybercrime. (Bohat se mumalik ne cybercrime ke khilaf kanoon banaye hain.)
5.  **Legislates:** The committee **legislates** on internet safety. (Committee internet safety par kanoon banati hai.)
6.  **Legislates:** The body **legislates** to control data usage. (Yeh idara data ke istemaal ko control karne ke liye kanoon banata hai.)
7.  **Legislating:** Experts are **legislating** for better cloud security. (Mahireen cloud security ko behtar banane ke liye kanoon saazi kar rahe hain.)
8.  **Legislating:** They are **legislating** to protect user data. (Woh user ka data mehfooz karne ke liye kanoon saazi kar rahe hain.)
9.  **Legislation:** New **legislation** will control how AI uses data. (Naya kanoon control karega ke AI data kaise use karta hai.)
10. **Legislation:** This **legislation** is important for cybersecurity. (Yeh kanoon cybersecurity ke liye ahem hai.)
11. **Legislator:** Every **legislator** must understand computer basics. (Har kanoon saaz ko computer ki bunyadi maloomat honi chahiye.)
12. **Legislator:** The **legislator** proposed a new digital bill. (Kanoon saaz ne ek naya digital bill pesh kiya.)
13. **Legislators:** **Legislators** are discussing robot rights. (Kanoon saaz robot ke haqooq par baat kar rahe hain.)
14. **Legislators:** Many **legislators** want to tax big tech companies. (Bohat se kanoon saaz bari tech companies par tax lagana chahte hain.)
15. **Legislature:** The **legislature** passed the cybersecurity act. (Idaray ne cybersecurity act pass kar diya.)
16. **Legislature:** The state **legislature** held a meeting on data privacy. (Idaray ne data privacy par meeting ki.)
17. **Legislative:** The **legislative** process for software is slow. (Software ke liye kanoon saazi ka amal sust hai.)
18. **Legislative:** They have a **legislative** duty to protect networks. (Unki kanooni zimmedari hai ke networks ko protect karen.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The **legislature** met to discuss new cyber laws. (Idaray ne naye cyber kanoon par baat karne ke liye meeting ki.)
2. **Legislators** are worried about deepfake technology. (Kanoon saaz deepfake technology ke bare mein pareshan hain.)
3. The new **legislation** aims to stop data theft. (Naye kanoon ka maqsad data chori ko rokna hai.)
4. It is difficult to **legislate** for global software. (Global software ke liye kanoon banana mushkil hai.)
5. The **legislative** body reviewed the security report. (Kanoon saaz idaray ne security report ka jaiza liya.)
6. Many countries have **legislated** against hacking. (Bohat se mumalik ne hacking ke khilaf kanoon banaye hain.)
7. The **legislator** argued for stronger encryption laws. (Kanoon saaz ne mazboot encryption kanoon ki himayat ki.)
8. **Legislating** for AI is a complex task. (AI ke liye kanoon banana ek pecheeda kaam hai.)
9. The parliament **legislates** for the whole country. (Parliament pure mulk ke liye kanoon banati hai.)
10. This piece of **legislation** protects cloud users. (Yeh kanoon cloud users ko hifazat deta hai.)
11. **Legislators** need technical advice for better laws. (Kanoon saazon ko behtar kanoon ke liye technical mashware ki zaroorat hai.)
12. The state **legislature** voted on the tech bill. (State ke idaray ne tech bill par vote diya.)
13. **Legislative** changes are needed for modern tech. (Jadeed technology ke liye kanooni tabdeeliyaan zaroori hain.)
14. They are **legislating** to improve network security. (Woh network security ko behtar banane ke liye kanoon bana rahe hain.)
15. The **legislature** passed the law unanimously. (Idaray ne kanoon ko ittefaq-e-raay se pass kiya.)

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

| Word | Meaning | Academic Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Legislate** | To make a law | Used when discussing tech policy. |
| **Legislation** | A law | Used for specific acts/bills. |
| **Legislator** | A person who makes laws | Used for politicians. |
| **Legislature** | The group that makes laws | Used for the parliament/assembly. |
| **Legislative** | Related to laws | Used to describe processes/duties. |

**Memory Trick:** Think of the word **"Legal"**. If something is "Legal," it follows the law. **Legislate** is the action of creating that "Legal" rule!
