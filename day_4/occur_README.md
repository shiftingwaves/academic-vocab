---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:** occur, occurred, occurring, occurs, reoccur, reoccurred, reoccurring, reoccurs
*   **Noun forms:** occurrence, occurrences
*   **Adjective forms:** (None commonly used in academic contexts)
*   **Adverb forms:** (None)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Occur (Verb):** To happen. (Koi cheez hona ya pesh aana.)
*   **Occurred (Verb - Past):** Happened in the past. (Pehle ho chuka hai.)
*   **Occurring (Verb - Continuous):** Happening right now. (Abhi ho raha hai.)
*   **Occurs (Verb - Present):** Happens generally. (Aam tor par hota hai.)
*   **Reoccur (Verb):** To happen again. (Dobara hona.)
*   **Reoccurred (Verb - Past):** Happened again. (Pehle dobara ho chuka hai.)
*   **Reoccurring (Verb - Continuous):** Happening again and again. (Bar bar ho raha hai.)
*   **Reoccurs (Verb - Present):** Happens again generally. (Aam tor par dobara hota hai.)
*   **Occurrence (Noun):** An event or something that happens. (Koi waqiya ya hone wali cheez.)
*   **Occurrences (Noun - Plural):** Many events. (Bohat sare waqiyat.)

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Occur:** 
    *   *Happen* (koi cheez hona)
    *   *Take place* (waqay hona)
*   **Occurrence:** 
    *   *Event* (waqiya)
    *   *Incident* (koi baat ya waqiya)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. Researchers studied why the error **occurs**. (Researchers ne parha ke error kyun hota hai.)

9. No such **occurrence** has been reported yet. (Abhi tak aisa koi waqiya report nahi hua.)

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

**Core Meaning:** To happen or take place.

| Word | Simple Meaning | Academic Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Occur** | To happen | Used for errors, events, or processes. |
| **Occurrence** | An event | Used to describe a specific incident. |
| **Reoccur** | To happen again | Used for recurring technical issues. |

**Memory Trick:**
Think of **"Occur"** as **"O"** (Open) + **"Cur"** (Current). When something is **current** and **open**, it **occurs** (happens) right now!
