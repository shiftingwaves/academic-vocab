---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Legal (Adjective):** Allowed by the law.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo law (qanoon) ke mutabiq ho, yaani sahi kaam.
*   **Illegal (Adjective):** Not allowed by the law.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo law ke khilaf ho, yaani ghalat ya jurm.
*   **Legality (Noun):** The state of being legal.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi kaam ka qanooni hona.
*   **Illegality (Noun):** The state of being illegal.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi kaam ka qanoon ke khilaf hona.
*   **Legally (Adverb):** In a way that follows the law.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Qanooni taur par.
*   **Illegally (Adverb):** In a way that breaks the law.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Qanoon toar kar ya ghalat tariqe se.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Legal:**
    *   **Lawful:** Qanooni.
    *   **Valid:** Jaiz ya sahi.
*   **Illegal:**
    *   **Unlawful:** Qanoon ke khilaf.
    *   **Prohibited:** Mana kiya gaya.

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Legal:**
    1.  We must use **legal** software for our office work. (Hamein apne office ke kaam ke liye legal software use karna chahiye.)
    2.  The company has a **legal** right to protect its code. (Company ke paas apne code ko protect karne ka qanooni haq hai.)
*   **Illegal:**
    1.  Downloading pirated games is **illegal**. (Pirated games download karna qanoon ke khilaf hai.)
    2.  The hacker performed an **illegal** entry into the server. (Hacker ne server mein ghalat tariqe se entry ki.)
*   **Legality:**
    1.  We are checking the **legality** of this new privacy policy. (Hum is nayi privacy policy ke qanooni hone ki jaanch kar rahe hain.)
    2.  The court will decide on the **legality** of the data collection. (Court data collect karne ke qanooni pehlu par faisla karegi.)
*   **Illegality:**
    1.  The report highlights the **illegality** of the data breach. (Report data breach ke ghalat hone par roshni dalti hai.)
    2.  He was warned about the **illegality** of sharing private keys. (Use private keys share karne ke jurm ke bare mein warn kiya gaya.)
*   **Legally:**
    1.  The software is **legally** owned by the developer. (Software qanooni taur par developer ki malkiyat hai.)
    2.  You are **legally** required to follow these terms. (Aap qanooni taur par in terms ko follow karne ke paband hain.)
*   **Illegally:**
    1.  The data was **illegally** accessed by an outsider. (Data ko bahar ke shakhs ne ghalat tariqe se access kiya.)
    2.  They **illegally** copied the database content. (Unhon ne database ka content ghalat tariqe se copy kiya.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1.  It is **illegal** to copy someone else’s research paper. (Kisi aur ka research paper copy karna qanoon ke khilaf hai.)
2.  The company operates **legally** in this country. (Company is mulk mein qanooni taur par kaam karti hai.)
3.  We must confirm the **legality** of this software license. (Hamein is software license ke qanooni hone ki tasdeeq karni hogi.)
4.  Sharing passwords is often done **illegally**. (Passwords share karna aksar ghalat tariqe se kiya jata hai.)
5.  Is it **legal** to use this open-source code? (Kya is open-source code ko use karna qanooni hai?)
6.  The **illegality** of the attack was proven in court. (Hamle ka qanoon ke khilaf hona court mein sabit ho gaya.)
7.  Developers are **legally** responsible for their apps. (Developers apni apps ke liye qanooni taur par zimmedar hote hain.)
8.  The website was closed for **illegally** selling data. (Website ko data ghalat tariqe se bechne par band kar diya gaya.)
9.  He questioned the **legality** of the new cyber laws. (Usne naye cyber laws ke qanooni hone par sawal uthaya.)
10. The firm provides **legal** advice for IT startups. (Firm IT startups ko qanooni mashware deti hai.)
11. You cannot **legally** change the copyright notice. (Aap qanooni taur par copyright notice ko tabdeel nahi kar sakte.)
12. Many people **illegally** download movies online. (Bohat se log online movies ghalat tariqe se download karte hain.)
13. The **legality** of AI-generated art is still debated. (AI se banayi gayi art ke qanooni hone par abhi bhi behas jari hai.)
14. Any **illegal** activity will result in account suspension. (Koi bhi ghalat kaam karne par account suspend kar diya jayega.)
15. They were **legally** allowed to use the database. (Unhein qanooni taur par database use karne ki ijazat thi.)

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

**Core Meaning:** Rules and Laws (Qanoon).

| Word | Simple Meaning | CS / Academic Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Legal** | Sahi/Qanooni | Software licenses, rights |
| **Illegal** | Ghalat/Jurm | Hacking, piracy |
| **Legality** | Qanooni hona | Rules checking |
| **Illegality** | Qanoon shikni | Breaking rules |
| **Legally** | Qanooni taur par | Following rules |
| **Illegally** | Ghalat tariqe se | Breaking rules |

**Memory Trick:**
*   **"Legal"** starts with **"L"** (Like **"Law"**). If it follows the **L**aw, it is **Legal**!
*   **"Il-"** at the start means **"Not"**. So, **"Illegal"** = **"Not Legal"**.
