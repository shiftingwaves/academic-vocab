### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   *To police* (to monitor or control)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   *Policy* (the main word)
    *   *Policies* (plural)
    *   *Policymaker* (a person who makes rules)
    *   *Policymaking* (the process of making rules)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   *Policy-driven* (based on rules)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   (None commonly used)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION (FOR EACH WORD)

*   **Policy:** A set of rules or a plan of action.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ye ek tarah ke usool ya planning hoti hai jo kisi kaam ko karne ke liye banayi jaati hai.
*   **Policies:** More than one policy.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jab ek se zyada usool ya rules hon.
*   **To police:** To check if people are following the rules.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez par nazar rakhna ke sab log rules follow kar rahe hain ya nahi.
*   **Policymaker:** A person who creates the rules.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh log jo rules ya policies banate hain.
*   **Policymaking:** The act of creating rules.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Policy banane ka kaam.
*   **Policy-driven:** When a system or action is controlled by rules.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jab koi kaam puri tarah rules ke mutabiq ho raha ho.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

1.  **Rule:** (Usool) - A guide for behavior.
2.  **Guideline:** (Hidayat) - Advice or a plan for how to do something.
3.  **Strategy:** (Hikmat-e-amli) - A plan to achieve a goal.

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Policy:** The university has a strict policy on plagiarism.
    *   *University ki plagiarism (cheating) ke baray mein sakht policy hai.*
*   **Policy:** Our software follows a data privacy policy.
    *   *Hamara software data privacy ki policy ko follow karta hai.*
*   **Policies:** The company updated its security policies last month.
    *   *Company ne pichle mahine apni security policies update ki hain.*
*   **Policies:** New policies are needed for Artificial Intelligence.
    *   *Artificial Intelligence ke liye nayi policies ki zaroorat hai.*
*   **To police:** The system is designed to police network traffic.
    *   *Ye system network traffic ko monitor (check) karne ke liye banaya gaya hai.*
*   **To police:** It is hard to police every user on the internet.
    *   *Internet par har user par nazar rakhna mushkil hai.*
*   **Policymaker:** The lead researcher acts as a policymaker for this project.
    *   *Lead researcher is project ke liye policymaker ka kaam karta hai.*
*   **Policymaker:** Policymakers are discussing digital laws.
    *   *Policymakers digital laws par baat cheet kar rahe hain.*
*   **Policymaking:** Policymaking in technology is very complex.
    *   *Technology mein policy banana bohot pecheeda (complex) kaam hai.*
*   **Policymaking:** Effective policymaking requires good data.
    *   *Behtar policy banane ke liye achay data ki zaroorat hoti hai.*
*   **Policy-driven:** This is a policy-driven network architecture.
    *   *Ye ek policy-driven network architecture hai.*
*   **Policy-driven:** We need a policy-driven approach to cyber security.
    *   *Hamein cyber security ke liye policy-driven tareeqay ki zaroorat hai.*

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The university **policy** prohibits sharing passwords. (University ki policy password share karne se mana karti hai.)
2. Many **policies** were changed to improve system security. (System security behtar karne ke liye bohot si policies badli gayi.)
3. **Policymaking** for the internet is a global challenge. (Internet ke liye policy banana ek global challenge hai.)
4. The IT department needs to **police** the use of private devices. (IT department ko private devices ke istemaal par nazar rakhni chahiye.)
5. This is a **policy-driven** system. (Ye ek policy-driven system hai.)
6. The **policymaker** signed the new document. (Policymaker ne naye document par sign kiya.)
7. We must follow the organization's security **policies**. (Hamein organization ki security policies ko follow karna chahiye.)
8. **Policymaking** involves many experts. (Policy banane mein bohot se experts shamil hote hain.)
9. The firewall helps to **police** incoming data. (Firewall aane wale data ko check karne mein madad karti hai.)
10. Does your company have a remote work **policy**? (Kya aapki company ki koi remote work policy hai?)
11. **Policymakers** are worried about data theft. (Policymakers data chori hone se pareshan hain.)
12. Our code is **policy-driven** to ensure safety. (Hamara code safety ensure karne ke liye policy-driven hai.)
13. Old **policies** are no longer useful. (Purani policies ab kaam ki nahi hain.)
14. The **policymaker** reviewed the new rules. (Policymaker ne naye usoolon ka jaiza liya.)
15. Strict **policymaking** can stop cyber attacks. (Sakht policy banane se cyber attacks ruk sakte hain.)
