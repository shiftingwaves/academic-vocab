Welcome to your academic vocabulary lesson! Today, we will learn about the word family **"Credit."** This word is very important in Computer Science (like in online systems or data) and academic writing (like giving respect to authors).

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Credit
    *   Credited
    *   Crediting
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Credit
    *   Credits
    *   Creditor
    *   Creditors
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Creditable (less common)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   Creditably (less common)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Credit (Verb):** To give praise or trust to someone.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi ko kisi kaam ka sehra dena ya maan lena ke usne ye kiya hai.
*   **Credited (Verb):** Past tense of credit.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi ko pehle se credit diya ja chuka ho.
*   **Crediting (Verb):** The act of giving credit.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi ko credit dene ka amal.
*   **Credit (Noun):** Recognition or praise.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Tareef ya izzat jo kisi ke kaam par milti hai.
*   **Credits (Noun):** Points earned in a course or units of value.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Wo points jo aap ko parhai ya kaam mukammal karne par milte hain.
*   **Creditor (Noun):** A person or company to whom money is owed.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Wo shakhs ya company jisko aap ne paise dene hon (qarzdar).
*   **Creditors (Noun):** Plural of creditor.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada log jin ko paise dene hon.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Credit (Verb):**
    *   Acknowledge (Kisi ke kaam ko tasleem karna)
    *   Attribute (Kisi kaam ko kisi ki taraf mansoob karna)
*   **Credit (Noun):**
    *   Recognition (Pehchan ya izzat)
    *   Approval (Manzoori ya tareef)

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Credit (Verb):** We must credit the original programmer for this code.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Humein is code ke liye asal programmer ko credit dena chahiye.
*   **Credited (Verb):** The discovery was credited to the research team.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ye daryaft research team ke naam ki gayi (credit di gayi).
*   **Crediting (Verb):** Crediting the source is necessary in academic writing.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Academic writing mein source ka naam likhna (credit dena) zaroori hai.
*   **Credit (Noun):** She deserves credit for the system design.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* System design ke liye wo tareef/credit ki haqdar hai.
*   **Credits (Noun):** You need 120 credits to graduate in Computer Science.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Computer Science mein graduate hone ke liye aap ko 120 credits chahiye.
*   **Creditor (Noun):** The company had to pay the creditor on time.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Company ko waqt par creditor (paise lene wale) ko payment karni thi.

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. Always credit the authors when using their research. (Research mein authors ko credit zaroor dein.)
2. He was credited with creating the first algorithm. (Pehla algorithm banane ka credit usay diya gaya.)
3. The university offers extra credits for research projects. (University research projects ke liye extra credits deti hai.)
4. The system is crediting the user for their contribution. (System user ko unki mehnat ka credit de raha hai.)
5. Creditors are waiting for the final payment. (Creditors aakhri payment ka intezar kar rahe hain.)
6. Proper crediting improves your academic reputation. (Sahi tareeqe se credit dena aap ki academic reputation barhata hai.)
7. The student earned many credits this semester. (Student ne is semester mein bohat se credits hasil kiye.)
8. We credit the success to our new software. (Hum is kamyabi ka credit apne naye software ko dete hain.)
9. Is the creditor satisfied with the report? (Kya creditor report se mutma'in hai?)
10. Crediting original work is a rule in research. (Research mein asal kaam ko credit dena ek rule hai.)
11. He was credited for his hard work in the lab. (Lab mein mehnat ke liye usay credit diya gaya.)
12. The project was credited to the senior developers. (Project ka credit senior developers ko diya gaya.)
13. You should check your total course credits. (Aap ko apne total course credits check karne chahiye.)
14. The company owes money to many creditors. (Company ne bohat se creditors ko paise dene hain.)
15. Crediting sources prevents plagiarism. (Sources ko credit dena plagiarism se bachata hai.)
