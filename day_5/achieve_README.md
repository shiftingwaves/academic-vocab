### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Achieve
    *   Achieves
    *   Achieved
    *   Achieving

    *   Achievement
    *   Achievements
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Achievable
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   (None commonly used)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Achieve (Verb):** To finish something successfully after hard work.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi kaam ko mehnat se pura karna ya maqsad hasil karna.
*   **Achieves (Verb):** Same as achieve, used for 'he/she/it'.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jab koi akela shakhs ya cheez maqsad hasil karti hai.
*   **Achieved (Verb):** Finished in the past.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo kaam pehle mukammal ho chuka ho.
*   **Achieving (Verb):** Doing the work to reach a goal right now.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi maqsad ko hasil karne ki koshish ya process mein hona.
*   **Achievement (Noun):** A success or a goal reached.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Koi bari kamyabi ya hasil kiya gaya maqsad.
*   **Achievements (Noun - Plural):** More than one success.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada kamyabiyan.
*   **Achievable (Adjective):** Something that can be done.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Wo kaam jo mumkin ho aur jise kiya ja sake.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Accomplish:** (Kaam pura karna)
*   **Attain:** (Hasil karna, jaise koi level ya maqsad)
*   **Reach:** (Wahan tak pohanch jana)

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Achieve:** Researchers hope to **achieve** better results with this new algorithm.
    *   *R.U:* Researchers umeed karte hain ke wo is nayi algorithm se behtar nateeja hasil karenge.
*   **Achieves:** This software **achieves** high speed by using less memory.
    *   *R.U:* Ye software kam memory use kar ke tez speed hasil karta hai.
*   **Achieved:** The team **achieved** its goal of launching the app on time.
    *   *R.U:* Team ne waqt par app launch karne ka apna maqsad hasil kar liya.
*   **Achieving:** **Achieving** high accuracy in AI models is very difficult.
    *   *R.U:* AI models mein high accuracy hasil karna bohot mushkil hai.
*   **Achievement:** Developing a quantum computer is a major scientific **achievement**.
    *   *R.U:* Quantum computer banana ek bari scientific kamyabi hai.
*   **Achievements:** His academic **achievements** helped him get a scholarship.
    *   *R.U:* Uski taleemi kamyabiyon ne scholarship lene mein uski madad ki.
*   **Achievable:** The target of 90% accuracy is **achievable** with more data.
    *   *R.U:* 90% accuracy ka target mazeed data ke sath hasil karna mumkin hai.

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. We must **achieve** our project goals before the deadline. (Humain deadline se pehle apna project goal hasil karna hoga.)
2. The student **achieved** a perfect score in the coding test. (Student ne coding test mein mukammal score hasil kiya.)
3. **Achieving** success in research requires patience. (Research mein kamyabi hasil karne ke liye sabar chahiye.)
4. This result is not **achievable** with the old system. (Ye nateeja purane system ke sath hasil nahi ho sakta.)
5. Her biggest **achievement** is creating a secure firewall. (Uski sab se bari kamyabi ek secure firewall banana hai.)
6. The AI **achieves** human-like conversation skills. (Ye AI insaanon jaisi baat cheet ki salahiyat hasil kar leti hai.)
7. Many scientific **achievements** started with a small idea. (Bohat si scientific kamyabiyan ek chote se khayal se shuru hui.)
8. Is it **achievable** to finish this code in one hour? (Kya ek ghante mein ye code mukammal karna mumkin hai?)
9. The team is **achieving** great progress in the lab. (Team lab mein bohot behtar tarakki kar rahi hai.)
10. He **achieved** fame for his work on data security. (Usne data security par apne kaam ke liye shohrat hasil ki.)
11. We need **achievable** milestones for our thesis. (Humein apni thesis ke liye aise maqsad chahiye jo hasil kiye ja saken.)
12. The processor **achieves** 3GHz speed easily. (Ye processor aasani se 3GHz ki speed hasil karta hai.)
13. Her **achievements** in CS are truly inspiring. (Computer Science mein uski kamyabiyan waqayi mutasir kun hain.)
14. By **achieving** this, we save a lot of energy. (Ise hasil kar ke hum bohot si energy bacha lete hain.)
15. The goal is hard but definitely **achievable**. (Maqsad mushkil hai magar yaqeenan hasil kiya ja sakta hai.)

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

| Word | Simple Meaning | Academic Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Achieve** | To reach a goal | Used for goals, targets, or results. |
