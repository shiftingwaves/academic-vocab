Hello! I am so happy to help you learn these important academic words. Today, we will learn about the word **"Conduct."** In Computer Science and academic research, this word is used very often. 

Let's start our lesson!

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Conduct
    *   Conducts
    *   Conducted
    *   Conducting
    *   Reconduct (less common)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Conduct (behavior)
    *   Conductor (a person or material that allows electricity to pass)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Conductive (able to carry electricity)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   None commonly used in academic writing.

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Conduct (Verb):** To organize or do a task or experiment.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi kaam ko perform karna ya koi experiment chalaana.
*   **Conducts (Verb):** To do a task (used for he/she/it).
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jab koi aik shakhs ya machine kaam karti hai.
*   **Conducted (Verb):** Did a task in the past.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo kaam pehle ho chuka ho (past tense).
*   **Conducting (Verb):** Doing a task right now.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo kaam abhi ho raha ho (continuous).
*   **Conduct (Noun):** How a person behaves.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi ka tareeqa-e-kaar ya bartaao.
*   **Conductor (Noun):** A material (like copper) that lets electricity flow.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Aisi cheez jo electricity ko apne andar se guzarnay de.
*   **Conductive (Adjective):** Something that lets electricity pass through it.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo cheez electricity ko pass hone de.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Carry out:** (Koi kaam pura karna)
*   **Perform:** (Kisi task ko anjam dena)
*   **Execute:** (Computer mein kisi command ko run karna)

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

1.  **Conduct:** Researchers must **conduct** experiments carefully.
    *   *Researchers ko experiments ehtiyat se karne chahiye.*
2.  **Conduct:** We need to **conduct** a survey on software usage.
    *   *Hamein software ke istemaal par aik survey karna hai.*
3.  **Conducts:** The system **conducts** a scan every hour.
    *   *System har ghante scan karta hai.*
4.  **Conducts:** The laboratory **conducts** tests on new hardware.
    *   *Laboratory nayi hardware par tests karti hai.*
5.  **Conducted:** They **conducted** a study on AI security.
    *   *Unhon ne AI security par study ki.*
6.  **Conducted:** The team **conducted** many tests on the server.
    *   *Team ne server par bahut se tests kiye.*
7.  **Conducting:** The students are **conducting** research on coding.
    *   *Students coding par research kar rahe hain.*
8.  **Conducting:** We are currently **conducting** a data analysis.
    *   *Hum filhal data analysis kar rahe hain.*
9.  **Conduct (Noun):** Professional **conduct** is important in research.
    *   *Research mein professional bartaao zaroori hai.*
10. **Conductor (Noun):** Copper is a good **conductor** of electricity.
    *   *Copper electricity ka acha conductor hai.*
11. **Conductive (Adj):** The material is highly **conductive**.
    *   *Yeh material bohat zyada conductive hai.*

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The lab is **conducting** a test on the new chip. (Lab nayi chip par test kar rahi hai.)
2. We **conducted** the experiment yesterday. (Hum ne kal experiment kiya.)
3. A good **conductor** is needed for this circuit. (Is circuit ke liye aik achay conductor ki zaroorat hai.)
4. The software **conducts** a security check automatically. (Software khud-ba-khud security check karta hai.)
5. Scientists **conduct** studies to find new solutions. (Scientists naye hal dhoondne ke liye studies karte hain.)
6. He **conducted** the interview with the software engineer. (Us ne software engineer ke sath interview kiya.)
7. We are **conducting** a review of the code. (Hum code ka review kar rahe hain.)
8. The metal plate is **conductive**. (Metal ki plate conductive hai.)
9. Bad **conduct** in a lab is not allowed. (Lab mein bura bartaao allowed nahi hai.)
10. Who **conducts** the final evaluation of the project? (Project ki final evaluation kaun karta hai?)
11. The team **conducted** an investigation into the bug. (Team ne bug ki investigation ki.)
12. **Conducting** research helps improve computer speed. (Research karne se computer ki speed behter hoti hai.)
13. The material is not **conductive** enough. (Yeh material itna conductive nahi hai.)
14. They **conduct** workshops on Python programming. (Woh Python programming par workshops karwate hain.)
15. The professor **conducts** his class via Zoom. (Professor apni class Zoom ke zariye lete hain.)

**Memory Trick:**
*   **Con-duct:** Think of a **Conductor** in an orchestra leading the music. Just like he "leads" the music, you "lead" an experiment when you **conduct** it!
