### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Acquire (Base form)

*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Acquisition
    *   Acquisitions (Plural)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Acquirable (less common)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   *None commonly used*

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Acquire (Verb):** To get something or learn a new skill.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ko hasil karna ya koi nayi skill seekhna.
*   **Acquires (Verb):** The same as acquire, used for 'he', 'she', or 'it'.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jab hum kisi single cheez ya person ki baat karte hain, toh 's' lagate hain.
*   **Acquired (Verb):** Something you got in the past.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo cheez aap ne pehle hasil kar li ho.
*   **Acquiring (Verb):** The process of getting something right now.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ko hasil karne ka amal (process).
*   **Acquisition (Noun):** The act of getting something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Hasil karne ka amal ya koi nayi cheez jo aap ne li ho.
*   **Acquisitions (Noun):** More than one thing that has been obtained.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada cheezein jo hasil ki gayi hon.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Obtain:** Hasil karna.
*   **Gain:** Fayda uthana ya hasil karna.
*   **Procure:** Kisi cheez ko dhoond kar lena (usually formal).
