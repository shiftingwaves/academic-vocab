### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Administrate (Verb):** To manage or organize a system or business.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi system ya kaam ko chalana ya manage karna.
*   **Administration (Noun):** The act of managing or the group of people who manage.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Intizamia; kaam chalane ka amal.
*   **Administrator (Noun):** A person who manages a system or software.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh shakhs jo system ko control ya manage karta hai (jaise System Admin).
*   **Administrative (Adjective):** Related to management or office work.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Intizami; jo kaam manage karne se talluq rakhta ho.
*   **Administratively (Adverb):** In a way that relates to management.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Intizami taur par.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

1.  **Manage:** (Sambhalna/Chalana)
2.  **Organize:** (Tarteeb dena)
3.  **Supervise:** (Nigrani karna)

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Administrate:**
    *   The IT team must administrate the server carefully. (IT team ko server ko ehtiyat se manage karna chahiye.)
    *   It is difficult to administrate such a large database. (Itne bade database ko manage karna mushkil hai.)
*   **Administration:**
    *   The university administration approved the new lab. (University ki intizamia ne nayi lab ko manzoori di.)
    *   Good administration is key to a stable network. (Behtar intizamia stable network ke liye zaroori hai.)
*   **Administrator:**
    *   The system administrator reset my password. (System administrator ne mera password reset kiya.)
    *   Only an administrator can access these files. (Sirf administrator hi in files ko access kar sakta hai.)
*   **Administrative:**
    *   She has many administrative duties in the lab. (Uske paas lab mein bahut se intizami kaam hain.)
    *   The team faced administrative delays in the project. (Team ko project mein intizami rukawaton ka samna karna pada.)
*   **Administratively:**
    *   The task was handled administratively by the head office. (Kaam ko head office ne intizami taur par handle kiya.)
    *   They are administratively responsible for the new software. (Woh naye software ke liye intizami taur par zimmedar hain.)
