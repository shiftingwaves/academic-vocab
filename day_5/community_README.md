### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Communalize (less common)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Community (singular)
    *   Communities (plural)
    *   Communalism (less common)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Communal
    *   Community-based
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   Communally

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Community (Noun):** A group of people who live in the same place or have the same interests.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek group ya log jo ek jagah rehte hain ya jinki pasand ya kaam ek jaisa ho.
*   **Communities (Noun - Plural):** More than one group of people.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ek se zyada groups ya logon ki jamaat.
*   **Communal (Adjective):** Something shared by a group.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Koi cheez jo sab log mil kar istemal karte hain.
*   **Community-based (Adjective):** Something that starts or happens inside a community.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo kaam kisi khaas group ya ilaqay ke logon ne mil kar shuru kiya ho.
*   **Communally (Adverb):** Doing something together as a group.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Mil-jul kar ya group ki shakal mein koi kaam karna.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Society:** A large group of people living together. (*Roman Urdu: Muashra*)
*   **Group:** A number of people or things. (*Roman Urdu: Giroh*)
*   **Association:** A group organized for a purpose. (*Roman Urdu: Tanzeem*)

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Community:**
    1. The computer science community is working on new AI tools. (*CS community AI tools par kaam kar rahi hai.*)
    2. He contributes to the open-source software community. (*Woh open-source software community mein hissa leta hai.*)
*   **Communities:**
    1. Researchers study how different communities use the internet. (*Researchers study karte hain ke mukhtalif communities internet kaise use karti hain.*)
    2. Online communities help students learn coding together. (*Online communities students ki coding seekhne mein madad karti hain.*)
*   **Communal:**
    1. The lab has a communal printer for all students. (*Lab mein sab students ke liye ek communal printer hai.*)
    2. This server is for communal use by the research team. (*Yeh server research team ke communal istemal ke liye hai.*)
*   **Community-based:**
    1. They started a community-based project for data security. (*Unhon ne data security ke liye ek community-based project shuru kiya.*)
    2. This app offers community-based solutions for farmers. (*Yeh app farmers ke liye community-based hal pesh karti hai.*)
*   **Communally:**
    1. The data is managed communally by the department. (*Data department ke zariye mil-jul kar manage kiya jata hai.*)
    2. We work communally to solve complex bugs. (*Hum mushkil bugs ko solve karne ke liye mil-jul kar kaam karte hain.*)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The research community is excited about quantum computing. (Research community quantum computing ko lekar bohot pur-josh hai.)
2. Many open-source communities share their code freely. (Bohat si open-source communities apna code free mein share karti hain.)
3. We need a communal space for team meetings. (Humein team meetings ke liye ek communal jagah chahiye.)
4. The project is a community-based effort to improve cybersecurity. (Yeh project cybersecurity behtar banane ke liye ek community-based koshish hai.)
5. Data is stored communally on the university cloud. (Data university cloud par communally store hota hai.)
6. Students learn faster when they work in a community. (Jab students community mein kaam karte hain toh jaldi seekhte hain.)
7. Different communities have different rules for data privacy. (Mukhtalif communities ke data privacy ke rules alag hote hain.)
8. The communal library provides free access to research papers. (Communal library research papers tak free access deti hai.)
9. This app is a community-based platform for developers. (Yeh app developers ke liye ek community-based platform hai.)
10. Developers solve errors communally on GitHub. (Developers GitHub par errors ko mil-jul kar solve karte hain.)
11. The tech community values innovation. (Tech community nayi soch (innovation) ko ahmiyat deti hai.)
12. Several communities joined the software development challenge. (Kai communities ne software development challenge mein hissa liya.)
13. Sharing resources in a communal way saves money. (Resources ko communal tariqe se share karne se paise bachte hain.)
14. Community-based learning helps in understanding complex CS topics. (Community-based learning mushkil CS topics samajhne mein madad karti hai.)
15. The team worked communally to finish the project on time. (Team ne project waqt par khatam karne ke liye mil-jul kar kaam kiya.)

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

**Core Meaning:** People/Groups working together or sharing resources.

| Word | Simple Meaning | CS / Academic Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Community** | A group | "The AI community" |
| **Communities** | Many groups | "Global research communities" |
| **Communal** | Shared by all | "Communal server/printer" |
| **Community-based** | Built by a group | "Community-based software" |
| **Communally** | Together | "Managed communally" |
