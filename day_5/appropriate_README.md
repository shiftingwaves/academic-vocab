Hello! I am your English language instructor. Today, we will learn about the word family **"Appropriate"**. This is a very important word in academic writing and Computer Science. It means "suitable" or "right for a specific situation."

Let’s start our lesson!

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Appropriate (to take something for your own use)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Appropriacy (the quality of being suitable)
    *   Appropriateness (the state of being right/suitable)
    *   Inappropriacy (the quality of being unsuitable)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Appropriate (suitable/right)
    *   Inappropriate (not suitable/wrong)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   Appropriately (in a suitable way)
    *   Inappropriately (in an unsuitable way)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION (FOR EACH WORD)

*   **Appropriate (Verb):** To take something (like money or property) for yourself.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ko apne istemaal ke liye le lena ya makhsoos kar lena.
*   **Appropriate (Adjective):** Correct or right for a situation.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo kisi kaam ke liye bilkul theek ya munasib ho.
*   **Appropriacy / Appropriateness (Noun):** The quality of being right.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ka munasib ya durust hona.
*   **Inappropriacy (Noun):** The quality of being wrong.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ka na-munasib ya ghalat hona.
*   **Inappropriate (Adjective):** Not correct or not suitable.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Jo munasib na ho, ya jo ghalat ho.
*   **Appropriately (Adverb):** Doing something in a correct way.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Munasib tareeqay se.
*   **Inappropriately (Adverb):** Doing something in a wrong way.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Ghalat ya na-munasib tareeqay se.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Appropriate (Adj):**
    1.  **Suitable:** Jo kaam ke mutabiq ho.
    2.  **Relevant:** Jo topic se talluq rakhta ho.
    3.  **Proper:** Jo sahi ya durust ho.

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Appropriate (Verb):**
    1. The government will appropriate funds for the new research lab. (Hukumat naye research lab ke liye paisay makhsoos karegi.)
    2. The company decided to appropriate the software code for their project. (Company ne faisla kiya ke wo is software code ko apne project ke liye istemaal karegi.)
*   **Appropriate (Adj):**
    1. Choose an appropriate algorithm for this data set. (Is data set ke liye munasib algorithm chunein.)
    2. It is appropriate to cite your sources in a research paper. (Research paper mein sources ka hawala dena munasib hota hai.)
*   **Appropriateness:**
    1. The professor checked the appropriateness of the study method. (Professor ne study method ke munasib hone ko check kiya.)
    2. We discussed the appropriateness of using AI in schools. (Hum ne schools mein AI ke istemaal ke munasib hone par baat ki.)
*   **Inappropriate:**
    1. Using informal language in a thesis is inappropriate. (Thesis mein ghair-rasmi zabaan istemaal karna na-munasib hai.)
    2. This data format is inappropriate for our database. (Ye data format hamari database ke liye ghalat/na-munasib hai.)
*   **Appropriately:**
    1. The system handles errors appropriately. (System errors ko munasib tareeqay se handle karta hai.)
    2. You must label your files appropriately. (Aapko apni files ko munasib tareeqay se label karna chahiye.)
*   **Inappropriately:**
    1. The data was handled inappropriately by the user. (User ne data ko ghalat tareeqay se handle kiya.)
    2. The software behaved inappropriately during the test. (Test ke dauran software ne ghalat tareeqay se kaam kiya.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. Always select an **appropriate** programming language for your task. (Apne kaam ke liye hamesha munasib programming language chunein.)
2. The team **appropriated** the unused budget for the CS project. (Team ne unused budget ko CS project ke liye makhsoos kar liya.)
3. It is **inappropriate** to copy code without permission. (Ijazat ke baghair code copy karna na-munasib hai.)
4. The server responded **appropriately** to the request. (Server ne request ka munasib jawab diya.)
5. We need to evaluate the **appropriateness** of this security protocol. (Hamein is security protocol ke munasib hone ka jaiza lena hoga.)
6. The user **inappropriately** closed the application while it was saving. (User ne application ko save hotay waqt ghalat tareeqay se band kar diya.)
7. Ensure the graph labels are **appropriate** for the audience. (Yaqeeni banayein ke graph labels audience ke liye munasib hon.)
8. The researcher **appropriated** the findings for his new article. (Researcher ne findings ko apne naye article ke liye makhsoos kar liya.)
9. An **inappropriate** choice of variables can cause errors. (Variables ka ghalat chunaao errors ka baais ban sakta hai.)
10. The system must be configured **appropriately** to avoid crashes. (Crashes se bachne ke liye system ko munasib tareeqay se configure hona chahiye.)
11. We questioned the **inappropriacy** of the proposed method. (Hum ne tajweez karda method ke na-munasib hone par sawal uthaya.)
12. Always use **appropriate** citations in your academic writing. (Apni academic writing mein hamesha munasib citations istemaal karein.)
13. The software was **inappropriately** installed on the server. (Software ko server par ghalat tareeqay se install kiya gaya tha.)
14. The **appropriateness** of the hardware is vital for performance. (Performance ke liye hardware ka munasib hona bohat zaroori hai.)
15. He **appropriated** the design ideas for his final project. (Usne design ideas ko apne final project ke liye makhsoos kar liya.)

**Memory Trick:**
*   **Appropriate** starts with "App" (like **App**lication). Think: "The **app** is **appropriate** for my work."
*   **Inappropriate** has "In" at the start, which often means "Not." So, **In**appropriate = **Not** appropriate.
