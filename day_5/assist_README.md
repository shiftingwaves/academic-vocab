Hello! I am your English language instructor. Today, we will learn about the word family **"Assist"**. This is a very important word in Computer Science and academic writing. Let’s start!

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:** assist, assists, assisted, assisting.
*   **Noun forms:** assistance, assistant, assistants.
*   **Adjective forms:** unassisted (less common).
*   **Adverb forms:** (None commonly used).

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Assist (Verb):** To help someone. (Kisi ki madad karna).
*   **Assists (Verb):** Helps someone (used for he/she/it). (Madad karta hai).
*   **Assisted (Verb):** Helped in the past. (Madad ki thi).
*   **Assisting (Verb):** Helping right now. (Madad kar raha hai).
*   **Assistance (Noun):** The act of helping; help. (Madad / Imdad).
*   **Assistant (Noun):** A person who helps. (Madadgar).
*   **Assistants (Noun):** More than one person who helps. (Ek se zyada madadgar).
*   **Unassisted (Adjective):** Doing something without any help. (Bina kisi madad ke).

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Assist:** 
    1. Help (Madad karna)
    2. Support (Sahara dena/support karna)
    3. Aid (Imdad karna)

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Assist:** This software will assist you in coding. (Yeh software coding mein aapki madad karega.)
*   **Assist:** The robot is designed to assist humans. (Yeh robot insano ki madad ke liye banaya gaya hai.)
*   **Assists:** A good interface assists the user. (Ek acha interface user ki madad karta hai.)
*   **Assists:** This tool assists in data analysis. (Yeh tool data analysis mein madad karta hai.)
*   **Assisted:** The professor assisted me with my research. (Professor ne meri research mein meri madad ki.)
*   **Assisted:** AI assisted the developers in finding bugs. (AI ne developers ki bugs dhundne mein madad ki.)
*   **Assisting:** He is currently assisting with the project. (Woh abhi project mein madad kar raha hai.)
*   **Assisting:** The app is assisting students with their homework. (Yeh app students ki homework mein madad kar rahi hai.)
*   **Assistance:** I need your assistance to fix this code. (Mujhe yeh code theek karne ke liye aapki madad chahiye.)
*   **Assistance:** Thank you for your technical assistance. (Aapki technical madad ka shukriya.)
*   **Assistant:** The research assistant gathered the data. (Research assistant ne data jama kiya.)
*   **Assistant:** My assistant will email you. (Mera assistant aapko email karega.)
*   **Assistants:** The lab has many assistants. (Lab mein bohot saare assistants hain.)
*   **Assistants:** The assistants are testing the new software. (Assistants naya software test kar rahe hain.)
*   **Unassisted:** The computer completed the task unassisted. (Computer ne bina kisi madad ke kaam pura kar liya.)
*   **Unassisted:** The drone flew unassisted. (Drone bina kisi madad ke uda.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The software assists researchers in complex calculations. (Software researchers ki mushkil calculations mein madad karta hai.)
2. I requested assistance from the IT department. (Maine IT department se madad maangi.)
3. A research assistant is required for this project. (Is project ke liye ek research assistant ki zaroorat hai.)
4. The AI assisted in identifying the patterns. (AI ne patterns pehchanne mein madad ki.)
5. He is assisting with the network setup. (Woh network setup mein madad kar raha hai.)
6. The system provides assistance for new users. (System naye users ke liye madad provide karta hai.)
7. Many assistants work in the Computer Science lab. (CS lab mein bohot saare assistants kaam karte hain.)
8. The program runs unassisted for hours. (Program ghanton tak bina kisi madad ke chalta hai.)
9. Can you assist me with this database error? (Kya aap is database error ko theek karne mein meri madad kar sakte hain?)
10. The teacher's assistants checked the code. (Teacher ke assistants ne code check kiya.)
11. Technical assistance is available online. (Technical madad online available hai.)
12. The robot assisted in moving the heavy parts. (Robot ne bhari hisson ko uthane mein madad ki.)
13. She worked as a teaching assistant for two years. (Usne do saal teaching assistant ke tor par kaam kiya.)
14. The script runs unassisted every night. (Script har raat bina kisi madad ke chalti hai.)
15. Your assistance in this study is appreciated. (Is study mein aapki madad ki qadar ki jaati hai.)

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

**Core Meaning:** To help or a person/thing that provides help.

| Word | Simple Meaning | CS / Academic Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Assist** | To help | To help a process or person. |
| **Assistance** | Help | Technical support or guidance. |
| **Assistant** | A helper | A person helping in research/lab. |
| **Unassisted** | Without help | Automation (working alone). |

**Memory Trick:** Think of **"Assist"** as a **"System"** that is **"Adding"** value to your work. A-S-S-I-S-T.
