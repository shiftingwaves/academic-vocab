### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

    *   Chapterize (to divide into chapters - *less common*)

*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Chapter-based (related to or organized by chapters)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   (None)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Chapter (Noun):** A main section of a book or a long document.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kitab ka ek hissa ya section.
*   **Chapters (Noun):** More than one section.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kitab ke mukhtalif hissay.
*   **Chapterize (Verb):** To arrange something into chapters.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi cheez ko hisson (chapters) mein taqseem karna.
*   **Chapter-based (Adjective):** Organized using chapters.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Wo cheez jo chapters ke hisab se bani ho.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Section:** Ek hissa ya bhag.
*   **Part:** Ek tukda ya hissa.
*   **Segment:** Ek alag hissa.

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

**Chapter:**
1. This chapter explains how the algorithm works.
   *(Is chapter mein bataya gaya hai ke algorithm kaise kaam karta hai.)*
2. Please read the first chapter before the lecture.
   *(Lecture se pehle bara-e-meharbani pehla chapter parh lein.)*

**Chapters:**
1. The research paper has five main chapters.
   *(Is research paper ke paanch ahem hissay/chapters hain.)*
2. These chapters cover different topics in Computer Science.
   *(Ye chapters Computer Science ke mukhtalif mauzoaat ko cover karte hain.)*

**Chapterize:**
1. We need to chapterize this long report for better clarity.
   *(Hamein is lambi report ko behtar samajhne ke liye chapters mein taqseem karne ki zaroorat hai.)*
2. It is difficult to chapterize such a complex project.
   *(Itne mushkil project ko chapters mein divide karna mushkil hai.)*

**Chapter-based:**
1. The course uses a chapter-based learning approach.
   *(Ye course chapter-based seekhne ka tareeqa istemal karta hai.)*
2. We followed a chapter-based structure for our thesis.
   *(Hum ne apni thesis ke liye chapter-based structure follow kiya.)*

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The first chapter introduces the basics of coding. *(Pehla chapter coding ki bunyadi baatein batata hai.)*
2. You must complete all chapters by next week. *(Aapko agle hafte tak tamam chapters mukammal karne honge.)*
3. The author decided to chapterize the manual for users. *(Author ne users ke liye manual ko chapters mein taqseem karne ka faisla kiya.)*
4. A chapter-based syllabus helps students study better. *(Chapter-based syllabus students ko behtar parhne mein madad deta hai.)*
5. Every chapter in this book includes a coding exercise. *(Is kitab ke har chapter mein ek coding exercise shamil hai.)*
6. I am currently writing the final chapter of my research. *(Main filhal apni research ka aakhri chapter likh raha hoon.)*
7. Please check the chapters listed in the table of contents. *(Bara-e-meharbani table of contents mein diye gaye chapters check karein.)*
8. The project is chapter-based to ensure steady progress. *(Project chapter-based hai taake kaam sahi raftar se chale.)*
9. Can you chapterize these research notes? *(Kya aap in research notes ko chapters mein divide kar sakte hain?)*
10. The second chapter focuses on database management. *(Doosra chapter database management par zor deta hai.)*
11. Reading the chapters in order is very important. *(Chapters ko tarteeb se parhna bohat zaroori hai.)*
12. This chapter-based guide is perfect for beginners. *(Ye chapter-based guide beginners ke liye zabardast hai.)*
13. The professor asked us to chapterize our findings. *(Professor ne hamein apni findings ko chapters mein likhne ko kaha.)*
14. Each of these chapters contains vital information. *(In mein se har chapter mein ahem maloomat hain.)*
15. We will discuss these chapters in the next seminar. *(Hum agle seminar mein in chapters par baat karenge.)*

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

**Core Meaning:** A distinct part of a larger written work.

| Word | Simple Meaning | CS / Academic Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Chapter** | Ek hissa | Used to divide a thesis or book. |
| **Chapters** | Ek se zyada hissay | Used to refer to multiple sections. |
| **Chapterize** | Hisse banana | Used when organizing research papers. |
| **Chapter-based** | Hisson par mabni | Used for course or project structure. |

**Memory Trick:** Think of a book like a **house**. Each **chapter** is a separate **room** in that house. You must go through one room (chapter) to get to the next!
