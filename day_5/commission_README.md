### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   Commission
    *   Commissioned
    *   Commissioning
    *   Commissions
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Commission
    *   Commissioner
    *   Commissioners
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Commissioned (Used as an adjective, e.g., "a commissioned report")
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   *None commonly used.*

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Commission (Verb):** To officially ask someone to create or do a piece of work.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kisi kaam ko karne ka official order dena ya task assign karna.
*   **Commission (Noun):** A group of people officially chosen to investigate something.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Logon ka ek group jo kisi khaas maqsad ya investigation ke liye banaya jaye.
*   **Commissioned (Verb/Adj):** When work has been ordered or paid for.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh kaam jiske liye order mil chuka ho ya payment ho chuki ho.
*   **Commissioning (Verb):** The process of starting a project or ordering work.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Kaam shuru karne ka amal ya order dene ki process.
*   **Commissions (Verb/Noun):** Plural of commission.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Zayada commissions ya kaam ke orders.
*   **Commissioner (Noun):** A person in charge of a commission.
    *   *Roman Urdu:* Woh officer jo commission ka head ho.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Authorize:** (Ijazat dena ya officially allow karna)
*   **Appoint:** (Kisi ko kaam par lagana)
*   **Task:** (Kisi ko zimmedari dena)

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Commission (Verb):** The university will commission a study on AI. (University AI par ek study karwayegi.)
*   **Commission (Noun):** The commission released a report on cyber security. (Commission ne cyber security par report jari ki.)
*   **Commissioned (Verb):** They commissioned a new software for the lab. (Unhon ne lab ke liye naya software banwaya.)
*   **Commissioning (Verb):** The commissioning of the new server will happen tomorrow. (Naye server ko start karne ka kaam kal hoga.)
*   **Commissions (Noun):** The government set up several commissions for IT growth. (Hukoomat ne IT growth ke liye kayi commissions banaye.)
*   **Commissioner (Noun):** The commissioner reviewed the data privacy policy. (Commissioner ne data privacy policy check ki.)

**Memory Trick:** Think of **"Commission"** as **"Official Task."** If you order a computer program, you "commission" it. If a group of people is officially tasked to look at a problem, they are a "commission."
