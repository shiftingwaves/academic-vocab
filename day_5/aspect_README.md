Let's start!

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:**
    *   (None common in academic English)
*   **Noun forms:**
    *   Aspect
    *   Aspects (plural)
*   **Adjective forms:**
    *   Aspectual (less common)
*   **Adverb forms:**
    *   Aspectually (less common)

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Aspect (Noun):**
    *   **English:** A particular part or feature of something.
    *   **Roman Urdu:** Kisi cheez ka ek hissa ya pehlu. Jab hum kisi badi cheez ko alag alag hisson mein dekhte hain.
*   **Aspects (Noun - Plural):**
    *   **English:** More than one part or feature of something.
    *   **Roman Urdu:** Ek se zyada pehlu ya hissay.

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Feature:** Koi khaas khasiyat ya hissa.
*   **Facet:** Kisi cheez ka ek pehlu (side).
*   **Element:** Kisi badi cheez ka ek zaroori hissa.

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Aspect:**
    *   Security is an important **aspect** of software development. (Security software development ka ek ahem pehlu hai.)
    *   The user interface is a key **aspect** of this application. (User interface is application ka ek ahem hissa hai.)
*   **Aspects:**
    *   We studied different **aspects** of Artificial Intelligence. (Humne Artificial Intelligence ke mukhtalif pehluon ka mutala kiya.)
    *   The research covers many **aspects** of data privacy. (Yeh research data privacy ke bohot se pehluon ko cover karti hai.)

---

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. This paper explores the technical **aspects** of cloud computing. (Yeh paper cloud computing ke technical pehluon ko explore karta hai.)
2. Speed is a critical **aspect** of database performance. (Speed database performance ka ek zaroori pehlu hai.)
3. We need to consider all **aspects** of the system design. (Hamein system design ke tamam pehluon par ghaur karna hoga.)
4. Design is a creative **aspect** of web development. (Design web development ka ek takhleeqi pehlu hai.)
5. Reliability is the most important **aspect** of this network. (Reliability is network ka sab se ahem pehlu hai.)
6. The study focuses on the social **aspects** of digital communication. (Study digital communication ke samaji pehluon par focus karti hai.)
7. Efficiency is one **aspect** that we cannot ignore. (Efficiency ek aisa pehlu hai jise hum nazar-andaz nahi kar sakte.)
8. The project covers two main **aspects** of coding. (Project coding ke do bunyadi pehluon ko cover karta hai.)
9. Every **aspect** of the code was tested. (Code ke har pehlu ko test kiya gaya.)
10. Privacy is a sensitive **aspect** of user data. (Privacy user data ka ek sensitive pehlu hai.)
11. He explained the theoretical **aspects** of the algorithm. (Usne algorithm ke nazriyati pehlu samjhaye.)
12. One **aspect** of the research is still incomplete. (Research ka ek pehlu abhi adhura hai.)
13. The team discussed various **aspects** of the new software. (Team ne naye software ke mukhtalif pehluon par baat ki.)
14. Compatibility is a key **aspect** of hardware integration. (Compatibility hardware integration ka ek ahem pehlu hai.)
15. Let us look at the practical **aspects** of this problem. (Chalo is maslay ke amli pehluon ko dekhte hain.)

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

| Word | Simple Meaning | Common CS Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Aspect** | A part or side of something | Discussing features of a system/code. |
| **Aspects** | Multiple parts or sides | Analyzing different parts of a project. |

**Memory Trick:**
Think of a **Diamond**. A diamond has many sides. Each side is an **aspect**. When you look at a computer program, you look at its "sides" (Security, Speed, Design). These are the **aspects** of the program!
