Hello! I am your English instructor. Today, we are going to learn about the word **"conclude"** and its family. These words are very important in Computer Science and research. Let’s start!

---

### 1. FULL WORD FAMILY

*   **Verb forms:** conclude, concludes, concluded, concluding
*   **Noun forms:** conclusion, conclusions
*   **Adjective forms:** conclusive, inconclusive
*   **Adverb forms:** conclusively, inconclusively

---

### 2. EXPLANATION

*   **Conclude (Verb):** To finish or decide something after thinking. (Kisi cheez ko khatam karna ya faisla karna.)
*   **Concludes (Verb):** He/she/it finishes. (Jab koi cheez end hoti hai.)
*   **Concluded (Verb):** Finished in the past. (Jo kaam khatam ho chuka ho.)
*   **Concluding (Verb):** The act of finishing. (Khatam karte hue.)
*   **Conclusion (Noun):** The final result or end. (Aakhri nateeja.)
*   **Conclusions (Noun):** More than one result. (Ek se zyada nateeje.)
*   **Conclusive (Adjective):** Something that is clear and final. (Bilkul saaf aur final baat.)
*   **Inconclusive (Adjective):** Not clear; no final result. (Jab koi saaf nateeja na nikle.)
*   **Conclusively (Adverb):** In a way that is final. (Aisi tarah se ke baat khatam ho jaye.)
*   **Inconclusively (Adverb):** In a way that is not clear. (Jab baat adhoori ya ghair-waazeh ho.)

---

### 3. SYNONYMS

*   **Conclude (Verb):**
    *   **Finish:** Kaam khatam karna.
    *   **Determine:** Faisla karna ya maloom karna.
*   **Conclusion (Noun):**
    *   **Result:** Nateeja.
    *   **Ending:** Khatma.

---

### 4. ACADEMIC EXAMPLES

*   **Conclude:** The researchers will conclude the experiment today. (Researchers aaj experiment khatam karenge.)
*   **Conclude:** We can conclude that this algorithm is fast. (Hum ye faisla kar sakte hain ke ye algorithm fast hai.)
*   **Concludes:** The paper concludes with a summary. (Paper ka ikhtitam summary par hota hai.)
*   **Concluded:** The team concluded the project last week. (Team ne pichle hafte project khatam kiya.)
*   **Concluding:** They are concluding the coding phase now. (Wo abhi coding ka phase khatam kar rahe hain.)
*   **Conclusion:** The conclusion of the study is very interesting. (Study ka nateeja bahut dilchasp hai.)
*   **Conclusions:** The researchers shared their final conclusions. (Researchers ne apne aakhri nateeje share kiye.)
*   **Conclusive:** We need conclusive evidence to prove this theory. (Is theory ko sabit karne ke liye humein solid/saaf saboot chahiye.)
*   **Inconclusive:** The test results were inconclusive. (Test ke nateeje ghair-waazeh/adhure thay.)
*   **Conclusively:** The data conclusively shows a performance boost. (Data saaf tor par performance mein izafa dikhata hai.)
*   **Inconclusively:** The meeting ended inconclusively. (Meeting ka koi saaf nateeja nahi nikla.)

### 5. MIXED ACADEMIC SENTENCES

1. The research concluded that Python is easy to learn. (Research ne ye nateeja nikala ke Python seekhna aasaan hai.)
2. Please write your conclusion at the end of the report. (Baraye meharbani report ke aakhir mein apna nateeja likhein.)
3. The experiment provided conclusive proof of the bug. (Experiment ne bug ka pakka saboot diya.)
4. The investigation remained inconclusive for months. (Investigation mahino tak ghair-waazeh rahi.)
5. We are concluding our work on the database. (Hum database par apna kaam khatam kar rahe hain.)
6. The paper presents several important conclusions. (Paper kayi ahem nateeje pesh karta hai.)
7. Can you conclude this task by tomorrow? (Kya tum ye kaam kal tak khatam kar sakte ho?)
8. The scientists acted conclusively to fix the error. (Scientists ne ghalti theek karne ke liye fori aur final qadam uthaya.)
9. The team discussed their conclusions in the meeting. (Team ne meeting mein apne nateeje discuss kiye.)
10. The test results were debated inconclusively. (Test ke nataij par koi final faisla nahi ho saka.)
11. He concluded his presentation with a demo. (Usne apni presentation ka ikhtitam demo ke sath kiya.)
12. The final conclusion was that the system is secure. (Aakhri nateeja ye tha ke system mehfooz hai.)
13. The evidence is not conclusive yet. (Saboot abhi tak saaf/mukammal nahi hai.)
14. They are concluding the software testing phase. (Wo software testing ka phase khatam kar rahe hain.)
15. The expert concluded that the code needs optimization. (Expert ne ye faisla kiya ke code ko behtar banane ki zaroorat hai.)

### 6. CSAVL REVISION SHEET

**Core Meaning:** To end, finish, or reach a final decision.

| Word | Simple Meaning | CS / Academic Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Conclude** | To finish / decide | Ending a paper or a project |
| **Conclusion** | The final result | The summary section of a paper |
| **Conclusive** | Very clear / final | Strong proof for a theory |
| **Inconclusive** | Not clear | When an experiment fails to give a result |

**Memory Trick:** Think of a **"Conclusion"** as the **"Close"** of a chapter. Both start with **"C"**!
